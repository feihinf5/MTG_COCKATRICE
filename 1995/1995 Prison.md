Note
- World Championships 1995 top 4 deck by Marc Hernandez
- Core lock with combine [**Winter Orb**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image) and [**Icy Manipulator**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image)
- Tap the opponent’s single untapped land during their upkeep, locking them out of main phase mana and restricting them to instant speed spells
- In early MTG, tapping an artifact shut off its continuous ability, so tap your own Winter Orb at the opponent's end step to fully untap your lands
- Though that rule is obsolete, errata to [**Winter Orb**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/838f66d8-4f2d-4418-958c-e79aee0ce8aa?format=image) ensures the trick still works today if it is tapped
- The lock renders the extra cards opponents draw from [**Howling Mine**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image) useless, creating a massive resource gap
- Deploy [**Black Vise**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image) to deal massive, repeated damage to opponents with bloated hands
- Synergy with [**Armageddon**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image) and [**Balance**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image), players would sacrifice all their lands to [**Zuran Orb**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image) before casting [**Balance**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image) for a total board wipe
