Note
- World Championships 1995 top 4 deck by Marc Hernandez
- Core lock with combine [**Winter Orb**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/d60ee90d-5f7b-4294-8d52-9744fada8d36?format=image) and [**Icy Manipulator**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/1eda936f-7691-4440-9b83-eb0c6035b109?format=image)
- Tap the opponent’s single untapped land during their upkeep, locking them out of main phase mana and restricting them to instant speed spells
- In early MTG, tapping an artifact shut off its continuous ability, so tap your own Winter Orb at the opponent's end step to fully untap your lands
- Though that rule is obsolete, errata to [**Winter Orb**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/838f66d8-4f2d-4418-958c-e79aee0ce8aa?format=image) ensures the trick still works today if it is tapped
- The lock renders the extra cards opponents draw from [**Howling Mine**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/a4df1be4-364e-4582-929a-05f2905f8ce6?format=image) useless, creating a massive resource gap
- Deploy [**Black Vise**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/196b83a1-2a25-498d-83b8-8faacd79909d?format=image) to deal massive, repeated damage to opponents with bloated hands
- Synergy with [**Armageddon**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0794bc35-a8e1-4268-87b2-af5483ca6e5e?format=image) and [**Balance**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/db3e219d-8e5e-44c7-97b3-2d489e2808b9?format=image), players would sacrifice all their lands to [**Zuran Orb**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/3a9d1082-a862-45d4-9e5e-392e879fead6?format=image) before casting [**Balance**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/db3e219d-8e5e-44c7-97b3-2d489e2808b9?format=image) for a total board wipe
