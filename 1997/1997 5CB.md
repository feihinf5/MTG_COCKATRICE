Note
- World Championships 1997 winning deck by Jakub Slemr
- Mono black, splashing just enough without risking mana screw to reap the absolute best benefits of other colors
- Built around highly efficient black 2-drop creatures like Fallen Askari, Black Knight, and Knight of Stromgald
- Backed by removal from Incinerate, Contagion, and Shadow Guildmage
- Man-o'-War or Nekrataal, allowing to develop your board while simultaneously removing a opposing creature
- Choking Sands chipping away at the opponent's life total while disrupting their mana base
- Artifacts & Enchantments handled by Disenchant(sideboard)and Uktabi Orangutan
- Counter-Burn countered using Hydroblast(sideboard) and Pyroblast(sideboard)
