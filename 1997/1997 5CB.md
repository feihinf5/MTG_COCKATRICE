Note
- World Championships 1997 winning deck by Jakub Slemr
- Mono black, splashing just enough without risking mana screw to reap the absolute best benefits of other colors
- Built around highly efficient black 2-drop creatures like [**Fallen Askari**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/00107210-313f-49c1-84ff-92628f75b764?format=image), [**Black Knight**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/a03b6221-2c85-44c0-82f1-b2b9e2c83c80?format=image), and [**Knight of Stromgald**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/2b87069b-ebaf-4705-b5da-446932af9b73?format=image)
- Backed by removal from [**Incinerate**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/409b2be8-5bb6-45e0-ab87-ca73b4e3a396?format=image), [**Contagion**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/00c8f94a-7690-47f5-b664-61411a32ab74?format=image), and [**Shadow Guildmage**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/ba3fc11e-db36-430c-920b-31195913c16a?format=image)
- [**Man-o'-War**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4dbf9bf9-75cd-4b25-a3a1-43b7e029700b?format=image) or [**Nekrataal**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/dba3e342-88b7-4692-a3f7-a3f56c0cf6b5?format=image), allowing to develop your board while simultaneously removing a opposing creature
- Clear blockers and mill your own graveyard using [**Earthquake**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/01bde909-899d-4efc-aac5-57b69fa764db?format=image) then use the accumulated cards to fuel [**Necratog**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/fb19c519-c09a-44a0-8d4b-ab6c15dabdef?format=image)
- [**Choking Sands**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e41c15fb-01a1-446e-9e88-71e8e95d9bce?format=image) chipping away at the opponent's life total while disrupting their mana base
- Artifacts & Enchantments handled by [**Disenchant**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/71e27cbc-d986-437a-9195-6f5e065a49a2?format=image)(sideboard)and [**Uktabi Orangutan**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/101c7d58-43cc-4ebd-87f1-2016fbff56dd?format=image)
- Counter-Burn countered using [**Hydroblast**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image)(sideboard) and [**Pyroblast**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/c342cac5-08ae-4428-9c2c-f6c5904e54d2?format=image)(sideboard)
