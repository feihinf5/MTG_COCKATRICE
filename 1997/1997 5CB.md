Note
- World Championships 1997 winning deck by Jakub Slemr
- Mono black, splashing just enough without risking mana screw to reap the absolute best benefits of other colors
- Built around highly efficient black 2-drop creatures like [**Fallen Askari**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image), [**Black Knight**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image), and [**Knight of Stromgald**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image)
- Backed by removal from [**Incinerate**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image), [**Contagion**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image), and [**Shadow Guildmage**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image)
- [**Man-o'-War**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image) or [**Nekrataal**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image), allowing to develop your board while simultaneously removing a opposing creature
- [**Choking Sands**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e41c15fb-01a1-446e-9e88-71e8e95d9bce?format=image) chipping away at the opponent's life total while disrupting their mana base
- Artifacts & Enchantments handled by [**Disenchant**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image)(sideboard)and [**Uktabi Orangutan**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image)
- Counter-Burn countered using [**Hydroblast**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image)(sideboard) and [**Pyroblast**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image)(sideboard)
