Note
- Asia Pacific Championship 1997 winning deck by Nathan Russell
- Roughly 30 creatures, including [**Mishra's Factory**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/aff8d4f1-eaad-4afb-9097-2afab133f707?format=image), engineered to defeat the dominant Blue-White counterpost deck
- [**Black Knight**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/47716cf4-f35c-4613-9686-4e00b5063408?format=image)'s Protection from White immune to [**Swords to Plowshares**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/8bd501ae-5814-4336-a45b-2b88cb85d29e?format=image) and unblockable by [**Kjeldoran Outpost**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e0769fc7-50b5-4b49-8aff-af04536288fb?format=image) soldier tokens
- [**Skulking Ghost**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/f8ca7e96-0545-4f36-85c0-944d5c0b760a?format=image) allow to easily sail over ground-based tokens
- [**Tombstone Stairwell**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/f8fe2f99-7ec2-490c-8ec3-aa2fb4680826?format=image) to maintain late-game, the zombie tokens it generated at instant speed effectively bypassed [**Wrath of God**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4566f6a3-4d25-4df0-84be-fe4201138955?format=image)
- Utilized [**Krovikan Horror**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e1f3cb1c-6bde-4b55-b5bc-5b64b56930f2?format=image) to sacrifice its own creatures in response to [**Swords to Plowshares**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/8bd501ae-5814-4336-a45b-2b88cb85d29e?format=image), ensuring they fueled the graveyard for [**Tombstone Stairwell**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/f8fe2f99-7ec2-490c-8ec3-aa2fb4680826?format=image) rather than being exiled
- [**Ritual of the Machine**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/537b4109-ae7c-451a-8576-97f817a70d75?format=image) has excellent synergy with [**Krovikan Horror**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e1f3cb1c-6bde-4b55-b5bc-5b64b56930f2?format=image) and [**Tombstone Stairwell**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/f8fe2f99-7ec2-490c-8ec3-aa2fb4680826?format=image) by sacrificing expiring zombie tokens or recurring creatures to permanently steal an opponent's creature
- [**Nevinyrral's Disk**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image) can blow up the entire board to dump creatures into the graveyard, instantly fueling a massive army of tokens for [**Tombstone Stairwell**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image) and maximizing [**Krovikan Horror**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image)'s return value
