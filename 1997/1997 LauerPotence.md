Note
- Pro Tour-Chicago 1997 winning deck by Randy Buehler
- Replaces [**Nevinyrral's Disk**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/ba5fcfc5-0715-4c6c-8325-3b54a138634e?format=image) with cheap red burn spells ([**Lightning Bolt**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/cb9b9a9d-ae4c-4e04-bf9d-cae48f01292c?format=image), [**Incinerate**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9c3f00af-010d-4485-b8b7-47400d99c496?format=image), [**Firestorm**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e674aa8a-668a-4345-95ee-73a0b87bbcb1?format=image)) to gain quick resistance against fast aggressive decks
- Utilizes the "Lake-Drain" combo ([**Lake of the Dead**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/aee806ce-effa-4244-9659-43246e944d80?format=image) + [**Drain Life**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/d89c1d2f-87a1-4463-af21-b837da3e7d74?format=image))
- Runs [**Knight of Stromgald**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/2b87069b-ebaf-4705-b5da-446932af9b73?format=image), [**Order of the Ebon Hand**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/60ffbb40-13c1-4d01-9421-95b2410d0d3b?format=image) and [**Ihsan's Shade**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/82351724-2814-4d9e-b065-bb72c761b2e7?format=image), all creatures have Protection from White, completely bypassing [**Swords to Plowshares**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/057d2410-30d3-4b7a-9dc3-f2512c1cf31c?format=image) and [**Kjeldoran Outpost**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e0769fc7-50b5-4b49-8aff-af04536288fb?format=image) soldier tokens
- Runs a full 4 copies each of [**Necropotence**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/54d7a0c1-efb4-4a8d-ad92-a96d43835052?format=image) and [**Demonic Consultation**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/8d727b9b-6114-414d-9172-16b6e1db41cc?format=image) to consistently deploying an early [**Necropotence**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/54d7a0c1-efb4-4a8d-ad92-a96d43835052?format=image) is critical
