Note
- World Championships 1997 top 4 deck by Janosch Kuhn
- Speed and control deck
- Used powerful removal like [**Swords to Plowshares**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/375fd2cb-443b-4be4-ad60-6d1a8e74f510?format=image) and [**Incinerate**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9c3f00af-010d-4485-b8b7-47400d99c496?format=image) to handle early threats, alongside countermagic like [**Force of Will**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9a879b60-4381-447d-8a5a-8e0b6a1d49ca?format=image) and [**Counterspell**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/aedbcbaa-40f0-485f-8427-778edc2d2ec0?format=image)
- Relied on [**Thawing Glaciers**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/6411a8c6-010f-4863-a0fa-bbebe09d5c34?format=image) to continually fetch lands and [**Impulse**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9d710a97-062f-4773-b6c6-8aeddeb3b6e8?format=image) to dig through the deck for answers
- Recursive damage from [**Hammer of Bogardan**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/f7285f52-5df0-4f90-9cf7-a57295d90fd4?format=image) and the burn spell [**Disintegrate**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/2e1dce58-c0e5-472e-a575-b8d4b5958b3a?format=image)
- [**Wildfire Emissary**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/6d99204c-b42d-48bc-9a93-fae5660665c7?format=image)'s Protection from white ability prevents it from being targeted by white removal and its 4 toughness keeps it safe from 3-damage spells
- [**Frenetic Efreet**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image) was used as an evasive, nearly un-killable chip-damage threat
