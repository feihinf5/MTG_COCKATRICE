Note
- World Championships 2004 top 4 deck by Manuel Bevand
- [**Krark-Clan Ironworks**] is used to sacrifice artifacts for mana, which then activates [**Myr Incubator**] to exile the remaining library and spawn a massive army of Myr tokens
- These tokens can be sacrificed for even more mana to burn the opponent out entirely using [**Goblin Charbelcher**] or [**Fireball**]
- Running an all-artifact land base allows [**Goblin Charbelcher**] to instantly kill the opponent by exiling the entire library
