Note
- World Championships 2004 top 4 deck by Manuel Bevand
- [**Krark-Clan Ironworks**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/c60174d6-1f9d-4870-b3db-34d6fcb3f6ab?format=image) is used to sacrifice artifacts for mana, which then activates [**Myr Incubator**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/31b0f9ef-7404-4e05-b759-aaf1ebcfcb31?format=image) to exile the remaining library and spawn a massive army of Myr tokens
- These tokens can be sacrificed for even more mana to burn the opponent out entirely using [**Goblin Charbelcher**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b6c37fc1-4842-4bc5-93ed-83fff9e420f2?format=image) or [**Fireball**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/967465c4-619a-4407-bffc-ffadb89726cd?format=image)
- Running an all-artifact land base allows [**Goblin Charbelcher**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b6c37fc1-4842-4bc5-93ed-83fff9e420f2?format=image) to instantly kill the opponent by exiling the entire library
