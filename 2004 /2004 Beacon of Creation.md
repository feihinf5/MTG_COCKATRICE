Note
- The Finals04 winning deck by Asahara Akira
- Quickly floods the battlefield with lands using [**Kodama's Reach**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/85d207ac-0680-47ef-85d9-4323c1321d6f?format=image) and [**Sakura-Tribe Elder**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/91c7707a-bae0-4196-bf26-d276f57b7369?format=image)
- Stall for time using tokens from [**Beacon of Creation**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5bc629de-18a3-4780-9df6-28ee54a34b81?format=image) then finish the game with [**Rude Awakening**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0164e2ed-b722-4a7d-a4fb-5401af008cbf?format=image) or by winning via combat with [**Blanchwood Armor**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/36a9d7b8-c77e-48f3-89ce-1d5a6dc735e7?format=image)
- Maximizes the mana advantage by maindecking [**Plow Under**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/27128f26-0d89-4962-96e8-5fa343031a26?format=image), often pairing it with [**Creeping Mold**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/19a053c7-650b-4b4a-b54d-082a194e3cc2?format=image) to increase resilience against control decks
- Repeatedly cast [**Plow Under**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/27128f26-0d89-4962-96e8-5fa343031a26?format=image) via [**Eternal Witness**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/c7e10ca7-1e5d-4224-82cf-798a4d436d72?format=image) to lock the opponent out of drawing new cards
