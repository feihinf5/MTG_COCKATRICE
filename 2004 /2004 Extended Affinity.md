Note
- Pro Tour-Columbus 2004 winning deck by Pierre Canali
- Minimizes creature mana costs to maximize [**Aether Vial**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/741c479b-5e92-4837-9673-9bc72aa11d26?format=image) to enabling tricky, instant-speed creature casting
- Unneeded copies of [**Aether Vial**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/741c479b-5e92-4837-9673-9bc72aa11d26?format=image) are sacrificed to power up [**Arcbound Ravager**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/72c1a731-7854-42b1-8719-ac3c2a269c1f?format=image)
- [**Aether Vial**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/741c479b-5e92-4837-9673-9bc72aa11d26?format=image) bypasses colored mana requirements, allowing to cheat out [**Disciple of the Vault**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/644359dc-3c4c-4291-876d-7390dc466877?format=image) even when black mana is completely unavailable
- [**Aether Vial**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/741c479b-5e92-4837-9673-9bc72aa11d26?format=image) and [**Disciple of the Vault**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/644359dc-3c4c-4291-876d-7390dc466877?format=image) were banned in the Extended format on September 2005 https://mtg.fandom.com/wiki/Banned_and_restricted_cards/Timeline#2005
