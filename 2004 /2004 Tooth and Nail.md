Note
- Grand Prix-New Jersey 2004 winning deck by Jeff Garza
- Casting [**Tooth and Nail**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/02f0067c-2d38-46bd-b52e-070c2ce424f0?format=image) with its Entwine for [**Platinum Angel**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/59bb5aee-b334-4c24-875b-56751d4add02?format=image) and [**Leonin Abunas**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/c5474bcc-4ffd-4b6d-98d0-891b766d5961?format=image) to soft-lock opponent
- Alternative cast [**Rude Awakening**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0164e2ed-b722-4a7d-a4fb-5401af008cbf?format=image) for leathal from lands
- [**Sylvan Scrying**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b4ff07b6-be9f-498f-9f36-cbd64f1b10cc?format=image) and [**Reap and Sow**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/ff6c4ae7-8509-47a2-bc28-d131b1e6676c?format=image) can aggressively tutor up all 4 copies of [**Cloudpost**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/2f28ecdc-a4f0-4327-a78c-340be41555ee?format=image)
- [**Duplicant**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/d48a96f2-738f-433f-bfae-fbf378832a3b?format=image) can exile an opponent's legendary or massive threat
