Note
- Grand Prix-New Jersey 2004 winning deck by Jeff Garza
- Casting [**Tooth and Nail**] with its Entwine for [**Platinum Angel**] and [**Leonin Abunas**] to soft-lock opponent
- Alternative cast [**Rude Awakening**] for leathal from lands
- [**Sylvan Scrying**] and [**Reap and Sow**] can aggressively tutor up all 4 copies of [**Cloudpost**]
- [**Eternal Witness**] to bring cards back to your hand
- [**Duplicant**] can exile an opponent's legendary or massive threat
