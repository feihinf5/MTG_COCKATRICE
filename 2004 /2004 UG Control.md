Note
- Grand Prix-Rimini 2004 winning deck by Domingo Ottati
- Cast [**Serum Visions**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4bc61952-88ba-447a-835a-f1e9643fcd0d?format=image) and [**Thirst for Knowledge**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0ff1f608-203e-4413-8753-37fc49731c87?format=image) to dig combo pieces, [**March of the Machines**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9bdcc966-7837-463f-ae26-1096f34ac8c0?format=image) or [**Rude Awakening**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0164e2ed-b722-4a7d-a4fb-5401af008cbf?format=image)
- Use [**Oxidize**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/1349969e-1cbe-4541-b3ed-154f5a39f1f0?format=image) and [**Viridian Shaman**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/932bf583-efd3-42e9-8a7b-7b3fe07fbcf0?format=image) to shatter key enemy artifacts immediately
- Keep mana open for [**Condescend**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e8303b80-e29a-46b8-90b0-c0cfe551b435?format=image) to counter early threats while using its Scry 2 to smooth out upcoming draws
- Drop [**Solemn Simulacrum**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/00f9955f-a522-47bf-b064-92dd21a76b18?format=image) to grab a basic land, block an attacker and draw a card when it dies
- Deploy [**Eternal Witness**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/c7e10ca7-1e5d-4224-82cf-798a4d436d72?format=image) to rebuy your best counterspells, bounce spells, or shattered removal pieces from the graveyard
- Use [**Echoing Truth**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4aefedd7-1bf5-4148-9084-d7d8f1138140?format=image) as a flexible safety valve to bounce non-land permanents that slipped past counters
- Dropping [**March of the Machines**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9bdcc966-7837-463f-ae26-1096f34ac8c0?format=image) against an artifact deck acts as a one-sided board wipe for their lands and zero-drop artifacts
- [**Solemn Simulacrum**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/00f9955f-a522-47bf-b064-92dd21a76b18?format=image) becomes a 4/4 under [**March of the Machines**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9bdcc966-7837-463f-ae26-1096f34ac8c0?format=image)
- Cast [**Rude Awakening**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0164e2ed-b722-4a7d-a4fb-5401af008cbf?format=image) with Entwine to untap all your lands and turn them into attackers, and swing for lethal
- [**Last Word**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/139d2ece-f656-4cac-8d77-b0f083f76c70?format=image) is in the deck precisely to protect the winning turn
