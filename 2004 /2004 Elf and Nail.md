Note
- 2004 United States National Championship winning deck by Craig Krempels
- Reuse [**Wirewood Herald**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/35724e9f-efa6-47e7-ab4d-7defe38ba576?format=image) and [**Wood Elves**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/da7aa861-556b-43a1-bf57-1efbbd438975?format=image) by bouncing them with [**Wirewood Symbiote**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/49488b76-abaf-4dba-b01f-7b418e4ff295?format=image) or sacrifice them to [**Skullclamp**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/55318397-de3c-47ea-a088-72a24df5c8fa?format=image) to draw cards rapidly
- Generate massive mana using [**Vernal Bloom**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/baabb73c-e3d9-4ba1-9963-986e09ef49a7?format=image) to cast [**Tooth and Nail**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/02f0067c-2d38-46bd-b52e-070c2ce424f0?format=image) for [**Darksteel Colossus**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/cbc27b24-f085-48b0-8757-cd11fbf25b91?format=image) or a disruptive combo of [**Kamahl, Fist of Krosa**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/150d5229-b1a5-42cf-bf6a-04d246f1124f?format=image) and [**Triskelion**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/8b997abd-8eae-4e87-97fc-36dde95ef8c7?format=image) to destroy the opponent's lands
- [**Skullclamp**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/55318397-de3c-47ea-a088-72a24df5c8fa?format=image) was banned in Standard format during June 2004 https://mtg.fandom.com/wiki/Banned_and_restricted_cards/Timeline#2004
