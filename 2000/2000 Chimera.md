Note
- World Championships 2000 top 8 deck by Nicolas Labarre
- Infinite mana combo deck utilizing [**Saproling Cluster**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5f50072b-aedd-4074-b1f7-f9ce477c26c2?format=image), [**Fecundity**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/54c93c11-448c-402d-ac55-8d5ab4c83d7b?format=image) and [**Ashnod's Altar**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4a0f8965-ecd3-4da2-80aa-9c5034c0cd3b?format=image)
- Spend one card from hand and one colorless mana to create a token with [**Saproling Cluster**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5f50072b-aedd-4074-b1f7-f9ce477c26c2?format=image)
- Sacrifice the token to [**Ashnod's Altar**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4a0f8965-ecd3-4da2-80aa-9c5034c0cd3b?format=image) to add two colorless mana and draw a card from [**Fecundity**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/54c93c11-448c-402d-ac55-8d5ab4c83d7b?format=image)
- Looping the above 2 steps generates a net profit of colorless mana each time
- This mana is then pumped into [**Whetstone**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/627805a0-535f-4cba-8176-a4de290b9c15?format=image) or [**Snake Basket**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/3a9bc174-1d10-49bf-af4f-2f6061b1796e?format=image) for an instant kill
- [**Academy Rector**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4367bc78-0912-4abd-8edd-bc792558d01a?format=image) is used to tutor for key cards and [**Yawgmoth's Bargain**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/86901bf2-7722-43f8-b879-7a30630371fa?format=image) as well as it enables a surprise kill by using [**Pattern of Rebirth**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9f23c4f4-a191-4225-a3b7-dab5b1462922?format=image) to fetch [**Serra Avatar**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/288b0976-78e8-4fbe-8607-2e55d8761d3e?format=image) for an immediate attack
