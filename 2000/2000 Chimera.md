Note
- Infinite mana combo deck utilizing [**Saproling Cluster**], [**Fecundity**] and [**Ashnod's Altar**]
- Spend one card from hand and one colorless mana to create a token with [**Saproling Cluster**]
- Sacrifice the token to [**Ashnod's Altar**] to add two colorless mana and draw a card from [**Fecundity**]
- Looping the above 2 steps generates a net profit of colorless mana each time
- This mana is then pumped into [**Whetstone**] or [**Snake Basket**] for an instant kill
- [**Yawgmoth's Bargain**] is exceptionally powerful in this deck
- [**Academy Rector**] is used to tutor for key cards and [**Yawgmoth's Bargain**] as well as it enables a surprise kill by using [**Pattern of Rebirth**] to fetch [**Serra Avatar**] for an immediate attack
