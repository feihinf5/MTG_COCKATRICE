Note
- Grand Prix Philadelphia 2000 top 4 deck by Scott McCord
- Uses [**Necropotence**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/54d7a0c1-efb4-4a8d-ad92-a96d43835052?format=image) to rapidly dig for [**Donate**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/7f6d8ce9-f8c8-45ad-b74c-97fba0e2982e?format=image) and [**Illusions of Grandeur**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/17eeeef2-2ced-42b8-a5e0-1095c9e13b02?format=image)
- Gaining 20 life from [**Illusions of Grandeur**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/17eeeef2-2ced-42b8-a5e0-1095c9e13b02?format=image) fuels further [**Necropotence**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/54d7a0c1-efb4-4a8d-ad92-a96d43835052?format=image) draws
- This massive card draw virtually guarantees assembling the full combo while easily leaving mana or pitch cards up for [**Force of Will**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9a879b60-4381-447d-8a5a-8e0b6a1d49ca?format=image)
- Requiring only a 2-card combo allows the deck to fully exploit the powerful tutoring capability of [**Demonic Consultation**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/8d727b9b-6114-414d-9172-16b6e1db41cc?format=image)
- Reached its absolute strongest state when fast mana options like [**Mana Vault**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/05e9fec4-1e0a-4206-ab2b-cc2543cba667?format=image) and [**Dark Ritual**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4ebcd681-1871-4914-bcd7-6bd95829f6e0?format=image) were still available
- [**Mana Vault**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/05e9fec4-1e0a-4206-ab2b-cc2543cba667?format=image) and [**Dark Ritual**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4ebcd681-1871-4914-bcd7-6bd95829f6e0?format=image) were banned in the Extended format during April 2000 and during April 2001, [**Necropotence**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/54d7a0c1-efb4-4a8d-ad92-a96d43835052?format=image) and [**Demonic Consultation**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/8d727b9b-6114-414d-9172-16b6e1db41cc?format=image) were subsequently banned in the Extended format as well https://mtg.fandom.com/wiki/Banned_and_restricted_cards/Timeline#2000
