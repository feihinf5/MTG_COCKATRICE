Note
- Asia Pacific Championship 2000 winning deck by Mori Masaya
- Uses mana creatures to accelerate out [**Tangle Wire**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/ad62f313-8a8a-4ffa-ada2-b12b76288729?format=image) and [**Plow Under**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/a30735c4-7f12-4db9-972b-9b7568a8ada8?format=image) for disruption, before closing out the game with [**Deranged Hermit**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/bf0e94c9-61c4-4cc0-b5ce-db62bc2660ee?format=image)
- Minimizes the self-inflicted impact of [**Tangle Wire**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/ad62f313-8a8a-4ffa-ada2-b12b76288729?format=image) by utilizing mana creatures and tokens from [**Deranged Hermit**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/bf0e94c9-61c4-4cc0-b5ce-db62bc2660ee?format=image) as cheap tap fodder
- [**Arc Lightning**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0c81ade7-0074-4447-ba2c-b16fa0f09ccb?format=image) wiping out multiple mana creatures with a single spell
- [**Pillage**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/d04eeaba-7db8-49e8-b775-fbe9c89413fd?format=image) was also incorporated to reinforce the mana-screw strategy
