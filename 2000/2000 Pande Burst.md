Note
- Grand Prix-Sydney 2000 winning deck by Gordon Lin
- [**Pandemonium**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5f618231-28bb-4cdd-b887-a8aa186814d5?format=image) and [**Saproling Burst**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/de3a293d-08d8-49e4-b0fa-91afa0a5591d?format=image) on the battlefield allow to deal 21 damage instantly by creating Saproling tokens
- [**Replenish**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/7fd2fe13-bbc0-42b7-bc42-3b51910ce118?format=image) is commonly used to bring both enchantments from the graveyard to the battlefield simultaneously
- Utilizing [**Intuition**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/c99f6785-e5a1-4fdc-9fb5-e1a372e7e848?format=image) significantly reduces the number of deck slots needed for combo pieces
- [**Replenish**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/7fd2fe13-bbc0-42b7-bc42-3b51910ce118?format=image) was banned in Extended format during March 2001 https://mtg.fandom.com/wiki/Banned_and_restricted_cards/Timeline#2001
