Note
- The Finals05 undefeated deck in Extended format by Kuroda Masashiro
- Uses high-quality creatures like [**White Knight**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/cb9cb8ed-7abb-4e71-b42f-5041dd0c0394?format=image), [**Silver Knight**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/93f559da-08ad-402d-8c6b-3050bce5867b?format=image) and [**Lightning Angel**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/6518d0c5-58ee-4089-bf19-5030d4319681?format=image) to execute a beatdown strategy
- Backs up the assault with [**Umezawa's Jitte**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/3b6e5956-f795-451b-bb24-56462d1ced27?format=image) and removal/burn spells like [**Fire // Ice**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/18303862-4726-4136-814f-157aa7006579?format=image) and [**Lightning Helix**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/1b2ecf55-c1cc-4b28-b7ce-e1b25305155e?format=image)
- Employs [**Meddling Mage**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/176f84c6-aa5e-449c-bd2b-cc91a898f0c7?format=image) as both an efficient aggro creature and a tool to shut down the opponent's key cards
- Uses [**Trinket Mage**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4c5a41ab-1840-4abb-a8bb-f0b1e7d1b450?format=image) to tutor for 1-mana or less artifacts like [**Pithing Needle**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/78eb9e1d-113e-45ff-8435-32ee42fa5631?format=image) or [**Engineered Explosives**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/R8492a272-e595-4f94-a6eb-08d29f211fd6?format=image) to lock down the opponent or artifact lands
