Note
- Grand Prix-Salt Lake City 2005 winning deck by Antonino De Rosa
- Draw-Go Control deck to deny the opponent's plays with counterspells, draw a massive hand and drop an unstoppable legendary creature to finish the game.
- [**Jushi Apprentice**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/33a8e5b9-6bfb-4ff2-a16d-3168a5412807?format=image) to draws extra cards early and flips into Tomoya to draw massive chunks of cards later
- [**Azami, Lady of Scrolls**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/15ea91b1-f2ff-4b99-8148-333aa27ea8cd?format=image) taps herself and other Wizards to draw multiple cards instantly, ignoring summoning sickness
- [**Minamo, School at Water's Edge**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/7536292c-da25-41c8-ba28-1e35758a7f3d?format=image) to untap legendary creatures and use their draw abilities twice in one turn
- [**Disrupting Shoal**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/15589745-4c0a-4edf-ad45-3b7fa45e70c5?format=image) "free" counterspell used to stop threats even have no mana left open
- [**Hinder**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/dc7befed-805b-4a02-a87d-7df3a95db8a0?format=image) counters spells and buries them at the bottom of the library to stop recursion
- [**Threads of Disloyalty**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/999440a5-9659-4218-8594-1950f1155975?format=image) to permanently steals the opponent's aggressive early-game creatures
- [**Pithing Needle**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/78eb9e1d-113e-45ff-8435-32ee42fa5631?format=image) is a 1-mana artifact that completely shuts down activated abilities of opposing cards
- [**Meloku the Clouded Mirror**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/1caaa733-6d4c-4e18-a64e-f95851c7063b?format=image) to bounces own Islands to generate an army of 1/1 flying tokens at the end of the opponent's turn
- [**Keiga, the Tide Star**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/ea83eaeb-cf82-406b-977f-2bac41925739?format=image) is a massive 5/5 flyer that permanently steals the opponent's best creature if it dies
- [**Miren, the Moaning Well**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/53d414f0-15ae-446b-a8d7-56c1b502740c?format=image) can sacrifices own Keiga in response to removal to gain 5 life and trigger Keiga's steal ability
