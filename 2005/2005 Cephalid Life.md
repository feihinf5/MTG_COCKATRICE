Note
- Grand Prix-Seattle 2005 top 4 deck by Nakamura Shuhei
- Cast [**Nomads en-Kor**] or equip [**Shuko**], both have 0-mana targeting abilities that can activate repeatedly at no cost
- Target [**Cephalid Illusionist**] repeatedly with [**Nomads en-Kor**] or [**Shuko**], every target mills 3 cards, putting entire deck into the graveyard
- Dump [**Sutured Ghoul**], [**Krosan Cloudscraper**], [**Dragon Breath**] and a reanimation spell into the graveyard
- Cast [**Reanimate**] or [**Exhume**] on [**Sutured Ghoul**]
- Exile huge creatures like [**Krosan Cloudscraper**] to make [**Sutured Ghoul**] massive then [**Dragon Breath**] automatically attaches from the graveyard to [**Sutured Ghoul**], giving it Haste and Trample for an immediate single-turn kill
- Target [**Daru Spiritualist**] infinitely using [**Nomads en-Kor**] or [**Shuko**], each targeting instance gives it +0/+2, resulting in near-infinite toughness then sacrifice [**Daru Spiritualist**] to [**Starlit Sanctum**] or [**Worthy Cause**] to gain millions of life, effectively locking out aggressive decks
- [**Living Wish**] fetches silver-bullet creatures or lands directly from the sideboard, including backup combo pieces or lock pieces like [**Dosan the Falling Leaf**]
