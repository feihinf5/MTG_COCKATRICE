Note
- Grand Prix-Seattle 2005 top 4 deck by Nakamura Shuhei
- Cast [**Nomads en-Kor**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b3a03c68-0ebe-488a-8e6c-7cbf7a448416?format=image) or equip [**Shuko**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/a47456b8-cef8-4085-90b1-92788e16fd27?format=image), both have 0-mana targeting abilities that can activate repeatedly at no cost
- Target [**Cephalid Illusionist**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/dceb8cf5-b31a-400e-aea5-ad0c3552d697?format=image) repeatedly with [**Nomads en-Kor**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b3a03c68-0ebe-488a-8e6c-7cbf7a448416?format=image) or [**Shuko**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/a47456b8-cef8-4085-90b1-92788e16fd27?format=image), every target mills 3 cards, putting entire deck into the graveyard
- Dump [**Sutured Ghoul**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/f769536f-def1-40b8-863f-ba12c7cb0d87?format=image), [**Krosan Cloudscraper**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/51ef4cda-e55b-45a8-9c02-4e77e5b15a9e?format=image), [**Dragon Breath**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/1832aaed-e164-4f78-9bc9-ec6c015835f5?format=image) and a reanimation spell into the graveyard
- Cast [**Reanimate**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/ae1ef31c-8ca5-444c-8f39-e1d1827318f5?format=image) or [**Exhume**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/a88b23ce-ce19-47da-b9f2-055a4d6bdc79?format=image) on [**Sutured Ghoul**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/f769536f-def1-40b8-863f-ba12c7cb0d87?format=image)
- Exile huge creatures like [**Krosan Cloudscraper**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/51ef4cda-e55b-45a8-9c02-4e77e5b15a9e?format=image) to make [**Sutured Ghoul**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/f769536f-def1-40b8-863f-ba12c7cb0d87?format=image) massive then [**Dragon Breath**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/1832aaed-e164-4f78-9bc9-ec6c015835f5?format=image) automatically attaches from the graveyard to [**Sutured Ghoul**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/f769536f-def1-40b8-863f-ba12c7cb0d87?format=image), giving it Haste and Trample for an immediate single-turn kill
- Target [**Daru Spiritualist**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/18f26b88-cffc-47ed-a70a-7d704a32c8bb?format=image) infinitely using [**Nomads en-Kor**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b3a03c68-0ebe-488a-8e6c-7cbf7a448416?format=image) or [**Shuko**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/a47456b8-cef8-4085-90b1-92788e16fd27?format=image), each targeting instance gives it +0/+2, resulting in near-infinite toughness then sacrifice [**Daru Spiritualist**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/18f26b88-cffc-47ed-a70a-7d704a32c8bb?format=image) to [**Starlit Sanctum**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/ace5e601-2583-4d9c-8bdf-aa33666c717c?format=image) or [**Worthy Cause**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/1f610d8b-1782-43a4-bfb3-40887bdedba0?format=image) to gain millions of life, effectively locking out aggressive decks
- [**Living Wish**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/2478a8d2-ca44-4c42-8d75-dd9cb1b59f61?format=image) fetches silver-bullet creatures or lands directly from the sideboard, including backup combo pieces or lock pieces like [**Dosan the Falling Leaf**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/ffb190db-48fc-4c39-ae9f-5e304eabb4f4?format=image)
