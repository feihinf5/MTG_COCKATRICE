Note
- Grand Prix-Singapore 2005 winning deck by Ishida Itaru
- Primary Finisher [**Psychatog**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/6757bf0e-489f-4be2-9e41-463b59f00dd1?format=image) and [**Wonder**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/44670666-9028-4b4a-a5af-a3bf35fc6a21?format=image)
- [**Brainstorm**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9ff71d13-c4b7-4125-ab10-db4abbb7a074?format=image) and [**Fact or Fiction**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/7fd4d018-dcf3-4439-8445-02d66e44f7d3?format=image) to rapidly filter libray deck and dump cards directly into graveyard to feed the [**Psychatog**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/6757bf0e-489f-4be2-9e41-463b59f00dd1?format=image)
- [**Foil**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/870fb793-3107-4cb2-ba78-34fbf5c9da2f?format=image), [**Force Spike**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/1d03d73f-0530-4125-8689-1c43e502e331?format=image), [**Mana Leak**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/56707af4-eb74-4b33-8741-6cc9b547d919?format=image) and [**Counterspell**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9a765377-bc8c-480a-9903-bd942c20fc47?format=image) to stop opponent from establishing a board
- Sometimes fetches [**Corpse Dance**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/76ae81ea-13e3-4ab8-b956-4c7b139a5e9c?format=image) via [**Cunning Wish**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/ca097675-5e82-493d-beab-9fc11efd7492?format=image) to push for the win
