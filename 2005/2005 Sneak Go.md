Note
- Grand Prix-Seattle 2005 top 8 deck by Fujita Tsuyoshi
- Fast-combo deck to generate massive mana on Turn 1 or 2 to drop an lethal creature via [**Sneak Attack**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/d07dc95d-82a8-4a58-8ea2-d4513bd7316d?format=image) or [**Through the Breach**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/6da09e6a-2965-4855-bd41-41b41ba188fb?format=image) to drop a massive creature with , pump it up and win immediately in a single attack
- [**Dragon Tyrant**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/04d1a29b-af80-4f9a-881b-ef7374ecbce1?format=image) with pitching high-cost cards to [**Blazing Shoal**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/8b915daa-d239-4460-bd6b-e1327fdf7f51?format=image) on this easily deals 20+ damage in a single swing
- [**Serra Avatar**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/288b0976-78e8-4fbe-8607-2e55d8761d3e?format=image) has power and toughness equal life total and capable of one-shotting the opponent directly
- [**Rorix Bladewing**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/7f2caba5-9f30-4b5e-833e-68c85a47ef7c?format=image) and [**Symbiotic Wurm**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/a60313ca-10cc-4c33-a557-1401c5721e3b?format=image) are secondary threats that provide immediate flying damage or leave behind 7 tokens if sacrificed
- Use [**Gamble**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0ee0f160-7339-4d98-8a8c-f08889ee52f5?format=image) to fetch whichever piece of the missing combo
