Note
- Grand Prix-Seattle 2005 top 8 deck by Fujita Tsuyoshi
- Fast-combo deck to generate massive mana on Turn 1 or 2 to drop an lethal creature via [**Sneak Attack**] or [**Through the Breach**] to drop a massive creature with , pump it up and win immediately in a single attack
- [**Dragon Tyrant**] with pitching high-cost cards to [**Blazing Shoal**] on this easily deals 20+ damage in a single swing
- [**Serra Avatar**] has power and toughness equal life total and capable of one-shotting the opponent directly
- [**Rorix Bladewing**] and [**Symbiotic Wurm**] are secondary threats that provide immediate flying damage or leave behind 7 tokens if sacrificed
- Use [**Gamble**] to fetch whichever piece of the missing combo
