Note
- 2005 Canadian National Championship winning deck by Jason Olynyk
- Black Green discard & aggro deck
- [**Ravenous Rats**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/a7b09e94-9504-444c-b002-7418962bf0f9?format=image) and [**Chittering Rats**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/980135d5-dfaa-4beb-b4b3-1e256bb46e61?format=image) force immediate card discard or lock the opponent's next draw step
- [**Nezumi Shortfang**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/c8265c39-d287-4c5a-baba-f2f09dd80a1c?format=image) forces repeated discard every turn, flipping into Stabwhisker once the opponent is hellbent (has 0 cards) to burn them for damage each upkeep
- [**Hypnotic Specter**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/205b6734-0205-4a57-ba55-2fd7ab40b8fa?format=image) forces a random discard whenever it connects, destroying the opponent's strategy
- [**Aether Vial**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/741c479b-5e92-4837-9673-9bc72aa11d26?format=image) Sits on the board and lets you flash in your Rats, Specters, or Shamans for free at instant speed, completely bypassing counterspells and ruining the opponent's combat math
- [**Blinkmoth Nexus**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/bf51c665-7823-4d6a-b1da-8c2d93dae10b?format=image) turns into a flying creature to safely carry equipment or activate Ninjutsu
- [**Umezawa's Jitte**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/3b6e5956-f795-451b-bb24-56462d1ced27?format=image) primary powerhouse that turns any tiny Rat or Specter into a lethal threat by generating counters to pump the creatures, kill opposing threats or gain life
- [**Ink-Eyes, Servant of Oni**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/386d391d-a3f8-49a4-802a-409944b2acf3?format=image) smuggled into play via Ninjutsu by bouncing an unblocked attacker and when she hits, she steals the best creature from the opponent's discard pile and puts it onto your battlefield
- [**Viridian Shaman**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/932bf583-efd3-42e9-8a7b-7b3fe07fbcf0?format=image) strictly to destroy opposing [**Umezawa's Jittes**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/3b6e5956-f795-451b-bb24-56462d1ced27?format=image) or [**Aether Vials**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/741c479b-5e92-4837-9673-9bc72aa11d26?format=image)
- [**Nekrataal**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/fc7d0ee9-3053-430a-a6a9-1202316f0648?format=image) and [**Rend Flesh**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/92b300a3-e6a8-4ca9-bb26-03f57b5ff6ec?format=image) to clear a path for your attackers
