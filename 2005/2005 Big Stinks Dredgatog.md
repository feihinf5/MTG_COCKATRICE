Note
- Grand Prix-Copenhagen 2005 winning deck by Julien Nujiten
- [**Psychatog**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/6757bf0e-489f-4be2-9e41-463b59f00dd1?format=image) grows exponentially by eating cards in hand and graveyard, delivering a lethal strike in a single turn, often made unblockable by giving it flying via [**Wonder**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/44670666-9028-4b4a-a5af-a3bf35fc6a21?format=image) in the graveyard
- [**Meloku the Clouded Mirror**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/1caaa733-6d4c-4e18-a64e-f95851c7063b?format=image) serves as an alternate, resilient win condition
- [**Gifts Ungiven**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/32b91eb5-ea53-4a21-a2e5-9c545a42fa30?format=image) choose cards like [**Life from the Loam**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/7ac16d09-8bc7-407c-a757-666f4707bc90?format=image), [**Genesis**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b43aee5e-b12e-43ea-9fae-16310acdc640?format=image) and [**Stinkweed Imp**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/628903a0-6695-4643-80f3-9a6efc4d6a27?format=image), later can access all of them anyway via Dredge or Genesis's recursion
- [**Life from the Loam**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/7ac16d09-8bc7-407c-a757-666f4707bc90?format=image) pairs with the "Cycling lands" ([**Lonely Sandbar**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/d8ddab06-aff7-4c40-bcaa-10cbfe899dd9?format=image), [**Barren Moor**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/45be3811-a223-4c45-9b24-0317f2d53c60?format=image)) to draw infinite cards and fuel [**Psychatog**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/6757bf0e-489f-4be2-9e41-463b59f00dd1?format=image)
- [**Deep Analysis**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/01e3c2e9-d8df-4a7a-be86-7be8c6254fa2?format=image) can be cast cheaply from the graveyard and [**Darkblast**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0dcaba91-06d3-4492-9e07-36a1b858ca47?format=image) offers repeatable removal
- Heavy counter-magic suite ([**Counterspell**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/29bb1b85-9444-4bfa-b622-092a6873631c?format=image), [**Force Spike**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/1d03d73f-0530-4125-8689-1c43e502e331?format=image), [**Mana Leak**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/56707af4-eb74-4b33-8741-6cc9b547d919?format=image) and the graveyard-synergistic [**Circular Logic**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/cd9198d6-201d-4175-8f70-eef92d7d5bb5?format=image)) slows down the opponent
- [**Smother**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9a8321af-d667-44e7-8c03-3957286604b9?format=image) provides early creature removal
- [**Mental Note**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/1f343724-6ecd-494f-8bfc-93676af4e173?format=image) fills the graveyard instantly at instant speed while replacing itself, accelerating and feeding Psychatog early
- Sideboarding transitions the deck to handle faster aggro or combo decks, using [**Duress**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/15c8d82e-6e65-4d36-bf09-b24dde016581?format=image) for hand disruption, [**Pernicious Deed**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/ae4cbb33-4947-49f0-b612-a92141fbfaa6?format=image) for mass removal and [**Coffin Purge**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/541e06cb-5616-40b4-9d36-89471a795ac8?format=image) for opposing graveyard strategies
