Note
- Grand Prix-Kitakyuushuu 2005 top 8 deck by Akihara Akira
- Sacrifices lands to fuel and transition into [**Balancing Act**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/7d6cb368-43f7-4d06-9cd5-492df5766567?format=image) then deploys [**Terravore**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/c39c412b-2f21-483a-b744-5d55bc007c0d?format=image) as the primary finisher
- Uses [**Burning Wish**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/1c9b692a-e832-4612-a6ec-93b52f6a0410?format=image) to guarantee highly consistent access to [**Balancing Act**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/7d6cb368-43f7-4d06-9cd5-492df5766567?format=image) from the sideboard while utilizing [**Life from the Loam**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/7ac16d09-8bc7-407c-a757-666f4707bc90?format=image) to easily recover sacrificed lands
- Utilizing [**Tooth and Nail**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/02f0067c-2d38-46bd-b52e-070c2ce424f0?format=image) to catch opponents off guard after sideboarding
