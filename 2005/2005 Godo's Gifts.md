Note
- Grand Prix-Niigata 2005 winning deck by Mori Katsuhiro
- Cast [**Gifts Ungiven**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/32b91eb5-ea53-4a21-a2e5-9c545a42fa30?format=image) to tutor [**Kokusho, the Evening Star**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/63dc0698-e92f-4134-80e4-5cc37b80e37c?format=image) or [**Ink-Eyes, Servant of Oni**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/386d391d-a3f8-49a4-802a-409944b2acf3?format=image), [**Goryo's Vengeance**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/3027e6c5-eed3-44e7-bb12-67569721af99?format=image), [**Soulless Revival**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/6b36712c-1ccb-4efe-8db8-823b9b80a99f?format=image) and [**Death Denied**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/8f66ddc5-f5e6-44de-8189-87b6521d1fea?format=image)
- Engine cards with the Arcane subtype allow to splice and recur removal indefinitely, [**Hana Kami**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/a8304b24-4db8-4082-bdb7-7010bf35e416?format=image) + [**Soulless Revival**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/6b36712c-1ccb-4efe-8db8-823b9b80a99f?format=image)
- Close out the game with repeated life drains from [**Kokusho, the Evening Star**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/63dc0698-e92f-4134-80e4-5cc37b80e37c?format=image), massive board clears via [**Kagemaro, First to Suffer**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/752f8672-1c37-4ab2-b290-fe3147784475?format=image) or spirit token floods with [**Meloku the Clouded Mirror**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/1caaa733-6d4c-4e18-a64e-f95851c7063b?format=image)
- Sideboard in [**Godo, Bandit Warlord**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/fcff0b92-edd0-4197-965b-3fd86bc884d8?format=image) along with [**Umezawa's Jitte**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/3b6e5956-f795-451b-bb24-56462d1ced27?format=image) and [**Tatsumasa, the Dragon's Fang**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/98d3bc63-8814-46e7-a6ee-dd5b94a8257e?format=image) which supported by adding the 1 [**Mountain**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/6e1a59c7-53d9-4e2c-9596-c64394838575?format=image), to tutor equipment directly onto the battlefield for huge tempo swings
