Note
- 2005 Vintage Championship winning deck by Roland Chang
- Lock deck using [**Mishra's Workshop**] as a powerful mana base and deploys lock pieces like [**Tangle Wire**] and [**Smokestack**] then recycles them with [**Goblin Welder**] to continually restrict the opponent's mana
- [**Trinisphere**] can achieve a complete lock on turn 1 and this oppressive power led to [**Trinisphere**] being designated as a restricted card https://web.archive.org/web/20060102095803/http://www.wizards.com/default.asp?x=dci/announce/dci20050301a
