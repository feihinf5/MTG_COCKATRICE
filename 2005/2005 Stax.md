Note
- 2005 Vintage Championship winning deck by Roland Chang
- Lock deck using [**Mishra's Workshop**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/135de5c7-6ac9-4b68-8f1a-97f120a4b125?format=image) as a powerful mana base and deploys lock pieces like [**Tangle Wire**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/ad62f313-8a8a-4ffa-ada2-b12b76288729?format=image) and [**Smokestack**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/6a37ab2d-094f-431e-878f-93aef0360413?format=image) then recycles them with [**Goblin Welder**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/6171e136-1167-4329-acb2-6853d3a814e5?format=image) to continually restrict the opponent's mana
- [**Trinisphere**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/d465597a-362e-4bd0-b547-f11d8807e597?format=image) can achieve a complete lock on turn 1 and this oppressive power led to [**Trinisphere**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/d465597a-362e-4bd0-b547-f11d8807e597?format=image) being designated as a restricted card https://web.archive.org/web/20060102095803/http://www.wizards.com/default.asp?x=dci/announce/dci20050301a
