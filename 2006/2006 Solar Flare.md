Note
- 2006 Argentina National Championship winning deck by Leonardo Calcagno
- Stabilizes the mana base using lands and Signets then reanimates high-impact finishers like [**Angel of Despair**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9ae71424-df9c-481c-9305-a0c30adcfda2?format=image) and [**Skeletal Vampire**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/69251d44-3038-4b64-ab78-fc87f0406ba2?format=image) to close out the game
- Deploys [**Wrath of God**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b6c1e9e3-c947-47cc-ac60-dc032d984a67?format=image) against beatdown decks and [**Persecute**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/54af13f6-7a7b-431d-9dda-6492d135563f?format=image) against control decks
- The inclusion of [**Court Hussar**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4970f492-d4fc-4833-a083-05e422a3c456?format=image) was crucial for early-game
