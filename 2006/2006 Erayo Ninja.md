Note
- 2006 Spanish National Championship winning deck by Javier Dominguez
- Uses flying creatures (like [**Birds of Paradise**]) to enable the Ninjutsu ability of [**Ninja of the Deep Hours**] or utilizes 0-mana artifacts to rapidly flip [**Erayo, Soratami Ascendant**]
- Employs [**Higure, the Still Wind**] as the primary win condition
- Can flip [**Erayo, Soratami Ascendant**] as early as turn 2 by playing a mana creature on turn 1, followed by [**Erayo, Soratami Ascendant**], a 0-mana artifact, and [**Repel**] on turn 2 to bounce and recast the artifact to hit the 4-spell threshold
