Note
- 2006 Spanish National Championship winning deck by Javier Dominguez
- Uses flying creatures (like [**Birds of Paradise**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/90a4396a-0f22-482b-ad1d-4d9b68a1ed96?format=image)) to enable the Ninjutsu ability of [**Ninja of the Deep Hours**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/367a67c7-54db-4336-b55a-3fa27625172a?format=image) or utilizes 0-mana artifacts to rapidly flip [**Erayo, Soratami Ascendant**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0b61d772-2d8b-4acf-9dd2-b2e8b03538c8?format=image)
- Employs [**Higure, the Still Wind**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/743f3cf5-f8aa-49d4-947d-76b91799547a?format=image) as the primary win condition
- Can flip [**Erayo, Soratami Ascendant**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0b61d772-2d8b-4acf-9dd2-b2e8b03538c8?format=image) as early as turn 2 by playing a mana creature on turn 1, followed by [**Erayo, Soratami Ascendant**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0b61d772-2d8b-4acf-9dd2-b2e8b03538c8?format=image), a 0-mana artifact, and [**Repeal**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9e7dd929-4bba-46a6-86c9-b8ed853eb721?format=image) on turn 2 to bounce and recast the artifact to hit the 4-spell threshold
