Note
- 2006 Canandian National Championship winning deck by Guillaume Cardin
- Play [**Heartbeat of Spring**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/6be3ba05-dce0-463b-bfe6-d51df63cc1a8?format=image) which causes all lands to tap for double mana and cast [**Early Harvest**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/22113d08-ecc9-4efd-90f1-00d699582ec3?format=image) to untap all basic lands, instantly doubling available mana pool
- Keep the chain going by using transmute cards like [**Drift of Phantasms**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/c1096ce5-f776-4028-b231-e6eaee35014b?format=image) (to tutor for [**Heartbeat of Spring**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/6be3ba05-dce0-463b-bfe6-d51df63cc1a8?format=image) or [**Weird Harvest**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/89bd2c25-049a-4df2-bf8f-198d1b41b01e?format=image)) and [**Muddle the Mixture**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4cc785b0-0a77-4b02-b0b4-2bda2fc621cc?format=image) (to tutor for [**Early Harvest**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/22113d08-ecc9-4efd-90f1-00d699582ec3?format=image))
- Cast [**Weird Harvest**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/89bd2c25-049a-4df2-bf8f-198d1b41b01e?format=image) for a large X value to tutor [**Maga, Traitor to Mortals**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/2563fd68-afdd-466f-8137-0769565674bf?format=image) to drain the opponent's life total to 0 instantly
- [**Invoke the Firemind**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/58d8e41a-5990-4ceb-9d41-76632faa7883?format=image) to alternatively, point a massive X-damage spell directly at the opponent
