Note
- Asia Pacific Championship 2001 winning deck by Okamoto Jin
- Uses [**Wrath of God**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0d223e83-0d3c-459e-96f5-ba9227fe49dd?format=image) and [**Story Circle**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/675378bb-7dc1-4bc8-b026-27e6e8e72e18?format=image) for defense, with a few copies of [**Millstone**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/1da21381-38bf-49d0-98d3-81eb9c99ce82?format=image) as the win condition
- Aggressive sideboarding that boards in additional finishers like [**Mahamoti Djinn**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5d464226-5607-4db2-bd43-7855efb92628?format=image)
- [**Last Breath**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/3b540da2-f8c6-48d6-af6d-db78958f0a17?format=image) to counter [**Lin Sivvi, Defiant Hero**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e574e522-2632-4cd4-8545-c582ac3b641f?format=image) and [**Nether Spirit**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/220217c5-408c-40df-8133-da16b13d4f21?format=image)
