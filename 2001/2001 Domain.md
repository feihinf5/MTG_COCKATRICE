Note
- Grand Prix-London 2001 winning deck by Kai Budde
- Maxing out basic land types makes [**Collective Restraint**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/d71daa57-ac02-4dd9-8c90-d38bdd45fb51?format=image) an incredibly powerful defensive tool
- Fully utilizes other Domain cards like [**Evasive Action**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5d0b4f29-ada4-41d2-8292-b5af537c6fd2?format=image) and [**Allied Strategies**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/51d4f211-10e8-486d-b982-287ab0c060c9?format=image) alongside heavy multicolor spells like [**Legacy Weapon**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/385d8691-b9bd-4b4d-86db-7a7cc6181104?format=image)
- Finishers typically favors threats with high removal resilience such as [**Ordered Migration**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/04d83a07-6054-45f1-bdf9-07f2006238d2?format=image) or [**Goblin Trenches**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/2100844c-6a41-40f5-b7f8-9b426d5a6945?format=image)
