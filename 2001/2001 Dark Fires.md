Note
- World Championships 2001 top 8 deck by Jan Tomcani
- Backed by mana accelerators like [**Llanowar Elves**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/ed8a8cfd-baef-4198-b1f3-4926139588b2?format=image) and [**Birds of Paradise**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/a2985857-fee5-42a6-9b5d-e157ada52a03?format=image), the deck deploys heavy hitters like [**Blastoderm**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9db5d6c2-b11f-442a-b172-c0c99c9bec07?format=image) and [**Saproling Burst**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/de3a293d-08d8-49e4-b0fa-91afa0a5591d?format=image) then using haste to rapidly beat down the opponent
- Turn 1 play [**Forest**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/695de19e-801f-4f08-b44c-b0726e4aced0?format=image) and cast [**Birds of Paradise**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/a2985857-fee5-42a6-9b5d-e157ada52a03?format=image)
- Turn 2 play [**Forest**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/695de19e-801f-4f08-b44c-b0726e4aced0?format=image) and cast [**Fires of Yavimaya**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/967f1658-8777-46fc-a648-07fb19e46745?format=image)
- Turn 3 play [**Forest**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/695de19e-801f-4f08-b44c-b0726e4aced0?format=image) and cast [**Blastoderm**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9db5d6c2-b11f-442a-b172-c0c99c9bec07?format=image) and attack
- Turn 4 Play [**Forest**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/695de19e-801f-4f08-b44c-b0726e4aced0?format=image) and cast [**Saproling Burst**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/de3a293d-08d8-49e4-b0fa-91afa0a5591d?format=image) to create three tokens then attack for lethal
- [**Spiritmonger**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b96d6e67-f690-4f19-bb25-a7c2d2aaf42f?format=image) push the deck's overall power level higher
