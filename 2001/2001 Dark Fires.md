Note
- World Championships 2001 top 8 deck by Jan Tomcani
- Backed by mana accelerators like [**Llanowar Elves**] and [**Birds of Paradise**], the deck deploys heavy hitters like [**Blastoderm**] and [**Saproling Burst**] then using haste to rapidly beat down the opponent
- Turn 1 play [**Forest**] and cast [**Birds of Paradise**]
- Turn 2 play [**Forest**] and cast [**Fires of Yavimaya**]
- Turn 3 play [**Forest**] and cast [**Blastoderm**] and attack
- Turn 4 Play [**Forest**] and cast [**Saproling Burst**] to create three tokens then attack for lethal
- [**Spiritmonger**] push the deck's overall power level higher
