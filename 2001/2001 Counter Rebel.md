Note
- 2001 German National Championship winning deck by Daniel Zink
- [**Lin Sivvi, Defiant Hero**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e574e522-2632-4cd4-8545-c582ac3b641f?format=image) features a flexible mana-cost recruitment ability alongside Rebel-exclusive graveyard retrieval
- [**Brainstorm**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9ff71d13-c4b7-4125-ab10-db4abbb7a074?format=image) put drawn Rebels back into the library, which pairs perfectly with the Rebel recruitment ability to shuffle them back in, especially useful if [**Rebel Informer**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/c98a71a8-291f-4d94-ada0-5f50f354cca7?format=image) is drawn
- [**Ramosian Sky Marshal**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/16638976-8a78-4233-8ebc-42ea9bb49e0a?format=image) is primary offensive creature
- [**Defiant Vanguard**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4c0bd267-59ec-41df-b0b7-37f6e6d6b073?format=image) is an effective blocker against [**Blastoderm**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9db5d6c2-b11f-442a-b172-c0c99c9bec07?format=image) and [**Chimeric Idol**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/fd0fb30e-e4d0-4271-a712-db40fa7650c3?format=image)
- Splashing [**Meddling Mage**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/176f84c6-aa5e-449c-bd2b-cc91a898f0c7?format=image) into the deck became highly popular
