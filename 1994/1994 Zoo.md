Note
- World Championship 1994 top 4 deck by Bertrand Lestree
- Beatdowns (like [**Kird Ape**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/ebe8845e-df1c-481c-949c-aab84af99a05?format=image)) with heavy burn spells (like [**Lightning Bolt**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/d573ef03-4730-45aa-93dd-e45ac1dbaf4a?format=image)) and explosive combos (like [**Channel**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image) + [**Fireball**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image))
- Mana base highly stable due to the inclusion of Dual Lands
- Leverages multi-color flexibility to run a full suite of Power 9
