Note
- Invitational 1999 played by Darwin Kastle
- Tap an [**Island**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/da1218e0-8c75-4f8e-b317-051ce84949a5?format=image) enchanted with [**Wild Growth**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/2a9e6630-d57c-47fd-af0f-b2e2c2025599?format=image) or any basic land enchanted with [**Fertile Ground**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/091dda35-59e5-456d-8804-61513a610aed?format=image) to produce 2 mana, including at least one Blue mana
- Activate [**Earthcraft**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9dda7531-82a1-4f49-8858-601ddbc6e2bc?format=image) by tapping [**Horseshoe Crab**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9b33646b-a0e3-4344-873e-6711743bc85c?format=image) to untap that enchanted land
- Spend the generated Blue mana to activate [**Horseshoe Crab**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9b33646b-a0e3-4344-873e-6711743bc85c?format=image)'s ability and untap itself
- Repeat the 3 steps above for infinite mana then cast a massive [**Stroke of Genius**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5e977755-8ea4-4a8b-90c4-dd175321e05d?format=image) to deck out opponent
- Synergy well with casting low-cost enchantments triggers draw engines like [**Argothian Enchantress**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9ababc1a-515e-4e20-8819-19d84d9b0af5?format=image)
- [**Time Spiral**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/f3d62dbd-63db-4ac9-950f-9852627f23f2?format=image) is used to untap all the enchanted lands while refilling the hand back to 7 cards
- [**Earthcraft**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9dda7531-82a1-4f49-8858-601ddbc6e2bc?format=image) was banned during March 1999 https://mtg.fandom.com/wiki/Banned_and_restricted_cards/Timeline#1999
