Note
- Grand Prix-Hiroshima 2003 winning deck by Azuma Motokiyo
- A near-lock deck built around [**Argothian Enchantress**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9ababc1a-515e-4e20-8819-19d84d9b0af5?format=image) and [**Words of Wind**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5595a57a-a76c-467b-afaf-5affffc24f35?format=image)
- Accelerates mana via various enchantments and [**Serra's Sanctum**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/f7a18130-dbaa-4657-a885-3a96a985935a?format=image) while drawing cards via [**Argothian Enchantress**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9ababc1a-515e-4e20-8819-19d84d9b0af5?format=image)
- Combo requires two or more [**Argothian Enchantress**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9ababc1a-515e-4e20-8819-19d84d9b0af5?format=image), one [**Cloud of Faeries**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4e76d04a-0038-4b5b-a026-3056ee940da9?format=image) and two enchanted lands with [**Wild Growth**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/ac714416-60ec-477d-b49f-83b3853a409f?format=image) capable of producing at least 6 mana
- Step 1, tap lands for mana and cast [**Wild Growth**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/ac714416-60ec-477d-b49f-83b3853a409f?format=image)
- Step 2, trigger draw from the [**Argothian Enchantress**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9ababc1a-515e-4e20-8819-19d84d9b0af5?format=image) and triggers [**Words of Wind**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5595a57a-a76c-467b-afaf-5affffc24f35?format=image) to bounce [**Cloud of Faeries**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4e76d04a-0038-4b5b-a026-3056ee940da9?format=image) and another [**Wild Growth**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/ac714416-60ec-477d-b49f-83b3853a409f?format=image) to hand
- Step 3, cast [**Cloud of Faeries**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4e76d04a-0038-4b5b-a026-3056ee940da9?format=image) to untap the lands
- Step 4, repeat above steps 1–3 for infinite mana and to bounce all opposing permanents
- Uses [**Living Wish**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/2478a8d2-ca44-4c42-8d75-dd9cb1b59f61?format=image) to fetch [**Ambassador Laquatus**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/887cac61-2001-4bd1-aeeb-20149ebc5856?format=image) to mill the opponent out with infinite mana
- Highly vulnerable to mass enchantment destruction, typically runs 4 copies of [**Lilting Refrain**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/ef319154-c5fc-4432-a860-a05508c132d4?format=image) in the sideboard to counter it
