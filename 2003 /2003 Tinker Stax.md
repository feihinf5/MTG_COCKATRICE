Note
- Pro Tour-New Orleans 2003 winning deck by Richard Osterberg
- Uses [**Tinker**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/7da23b15-dfb8-4267-9b33-d7a4c035c434?format=image) to tutor out oppressive control artifacts like [**Tangle Wire**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/ad62f313-8a8a-4ffa-ada2-b12b76288729?format=image) or [**Mindslaver**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/98fb1eaa-2871-491a-a4f5-3e358778ba40?format=image) then recycles them using [**Goblin Welder**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/6171e136-1167-4329-acb2-6853d3a814e5?format=image)
- The ultimate goal is to assemble [**Goblin Welder**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/6171e136-1167-4329-acb2-6853d3a814e5?format=image), [**Mindslaver**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/98fb1eaa-2871-491a-a4f5-3e358778ba40?format=image) and [**Pentavus**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/32a11f0a-7547-4fda-a8ed-caf76ce98f10?format=image) to establish a permanent, infinite turn-lock over the opponent
- [**Thirst for Knowledge**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0ff1f608-203e-4413-8753-37fc49731c87?format=image) serves as a discard outlet to set up quick artifact reanimation via [**Goblin Welder**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/6171e136-1167-4329-acb2-6853d3a814e5?format=image)
- Potential turn-3 kill, turn 1, play [**Ancient Tomb**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/30e401e3-282b-4524-87e1-c6cd50cd6d00?format=image) and deploy [**Lightning Greaves**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/61a28870-cf78-4323-9d82-cee764067764?format=image)
- Potential turn-3 kill, turn 2, play and sacrifice [**Seat of the Synod**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5da5587d-6b6c-4645-8cc9-2866d1e6911b?format=image) to cast [**Tinker**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/7da23b15-dfb8-4267-9b33-d7a4c035c434?format=image), tutoring [**Bosh, Iron Golem**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9bfe325c-8d3d-4543-9fcd-214525d4ab2a?format=image) directly onto the battlefield then equip the [**Lightning Greaves**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/61a28870-cf78-4323-9d82-cee764067764?format=image) and swing for 6
- Potential turn-3 kill, turn 3, play red-mana land or artifact then attack with [**Bosh, Iron Golem**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9bfe325c-8d3d-4543-9fcd-214525d4ab2a?format=image) again (totaling 12 combat damage) and use red-mana and [**Grim Monolith**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9ddc9fe1-17c8-4e1d-aeb8-c4214e881280?format=image) to activate [**Bosh, Iron Golem**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9bfe325c-8d3d-4543-9fcd-214525d4ab2a?format=image)'s ability, by sacrificing [**Bosh, Iron Golem**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9bfe325c-8d3d-4543-9fcd-214525d4ab2a?format=image) itself to deal 8 direct damage, reaching exactly 20 damage for the win
- Sideboard artifact destruction is for the mirror match
- [**Tinker**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/7da23b15-dfb8-4267-9b33-d7a4c035c434?format=image), [**Ancient Tomb**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/30e401e3-282b-4524-87e1-c6cd50cd6d00?format=image) and [**Grim Monolith**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9ddc9fe1-17c8-4e1d-aeb8-c4214e881280?format=image) were swiftly banned from the Extended format during December 2003 https://mtg.fandom.com/wiki/Banned_and_restricted_cards/Timeline#2003
