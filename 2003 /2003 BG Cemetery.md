Note
- 2003 Canadian National Championship winning deck by Josh Rider
- Control beatdown deck to utilize [**Oversold Cemetery**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/3bbfd715-0772-4516-8cd8-89495dbccf4a?format=image) for recurring powerful creatures like [**Nantuko Shade**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/2ed9dc9c-b92b-4305-8c54-1a63f750f8d1?format=image) and synergizes perfectly with sacrifice-based activated abilities, such as [**Ravenous Baloth**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/c98182d6-5b25-4493-9286-f29633e1bec4?format=image) and [**Stronghold Assassin**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/f388ad92-fa79-4a1a-b75c-e340dba016f4?format=image)
- Enables a "silver bullet" toolbox strategy using [**Living Wish**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/2478a8d2-ca44-4c42-8d75-dd9cb1b59f61?format=image)
