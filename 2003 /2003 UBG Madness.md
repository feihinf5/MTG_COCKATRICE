Note
- 2003 Japan National Championship winning deck by Ootsuka Koutarou
- Utilized [**Smother**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9a8321af-d667-44e7-8c03-3957286604b9?format=image) to destroy [**Psychatog**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/6757bf0e-489f-4be2-9e41-463b59f00dd1?format=image) and [**Engineered Plague**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b669e43e-3b11-42c9-8f20-0acce129e63c?format=image) to shut down Goblin and Elf aggro strategies
- Leveraged black cards not just for removal but also for [**Zombie Infestation**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/ccd5f98a-7ab5-44b3-850c-b50963dace66?format=image) to combo by running [**Upheaval**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9e201229-34a6-48c8-a07c-d8aefcf5f8a7?format=image) to float mana, bounce the board, and immediately flood it with Zombie tokens
