Note
- 2003 German National Championship winning deck by Dirk Baberowski
- Achieved tournament-level status by gaining [**Timberwatch Elf**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/045ae4ec-07f2-4098-a2d9-4bfcbd0273b2?format=image) and [**Caller of the Claw**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/a073459e-1f00-47e0-a1b3-d30203aa35d1?format=image)
- Accelerate mana with [**Llanowar Elves**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/ed8a8cfd-baef-4198-b1f3-4926139588b2?format=image) then flood the board with Elves at high speed and swing
- Uses [**Timberwatch Elf**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/045ae4ec-07f2-4098-a2d9-4bfcbd0273b2?format=image) for targeted pumps and [**Coat of Arms**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9642852b-8736-4fce-9f91-37594cbc3f71?format=image) for a massive, game-ending board buff
- Utilizes the [**Wirewood Herald**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/35724e9f-efa6-47e7-ab4d-7defe38ba576?format=image) into [**Caller of the Claw**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/a073459e-1f00-47e0-a1b3-d30203aa35d1?format=image) combo to survive and recover from mass removal
- Splashing red in the sideboard for [**Flaring Pain**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/eeb5c96a-1d16-459d-9968-ced9a8f1c520?format=image) for Wake deck and [**Goblin Sharpshooter**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/7e689df7-b85d-4346-bee8-5e978b5cbbbc?format=image) to defeat [**Phantom Centaur**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/c421c2f1-2137-41bd-9a89-74d8a76fb5c5?format=image)
