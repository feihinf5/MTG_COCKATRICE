Note
- World Championships 2003 winning deck by Daniel Zink
- Use [**Krosan Verge**] to get [**Mirari's Wake**] onto the battlefield by turn 4
- From the next turn onward, use the abundant mana to cycle cards with [**Compulsion**] and counter threats with [**Memory Lapse**]
- Copy [**Cunning Wish**] with [**Mirari's Wake**] to fetch another [**Cunning Wish**] and any other instant from outside the game then by using the flashback cost of [**Flash of Insight**], any blue instant can be recycled infinitely under the rules of that era
- Fetch [**Elephant Ambush**] from the sideboard using [**Cunning Wish**], cast it via flashback, and repeat the loop to generate an army of Elephant tokens to win.
- Combining [**Mirari's Wake**], [**Cunning Wish**] and [**Moment's Peace**] completely shuts down aggro and beatdown decks
- [**Decree of Justice**] revolutionized the deck that synergized perfectly with [**Mirari's Wake**] to easily create over ten 2/2 tokens and because it used the cycling mechanic, it was incredibly difficult for opponents to counter
- Historically, cards "removed from the game" (via [**Cunning Wish**]'s own effect or flashback) were still considered to be "outside the game" and this allowed [**Cunning Wish**] to return those removed instants directly back to the hand however under modern rules, the "removed from the game" zone has been renamed to the exile zone, which is officially part of the game so Because it is no longer considered "outside the game," no longer can use [**Cunning Wish**] to retrieve exiled cards
