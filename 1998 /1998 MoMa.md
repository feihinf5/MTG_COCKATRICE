Note
- The Finals 1998 winning deck by Koyami Tadayoshi
- Deploy low-cost mana artifacts such as [**Mana Vault**] and [**Lotus Petal**] to enable [**Tolarian Academy**] to generate massive amounts of mana
- Cast [**Windfall**] and [**Time Spiral**] to completely replenish the hand
- Use [**Mind Over Matter**] to discard the newly drawn cards, untap [**Tolarian Academy**] and fuel an even larger pool of mana
- [**Stroke of Genius**] as a draw spell and a mill-out weapon against the opponent
- [**Fireball**] is heavily utilized as insurance against "tower decks" (massive decks immune to mill) or situations where generating the full 60 mana for a mill-kill is impossible
- [**Pyroblast**] effective not only in mirror matches but also as a direct answer to hate cards like [**Arcane Laboratory**]
- [**Wasteland**] specifically chosen to disrupt opposing strategies in the mirror match
