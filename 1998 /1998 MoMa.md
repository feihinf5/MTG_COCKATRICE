Note
- The Finals 1998 winning deck by Koyami Tadayoshi
- Deploy low-cost mana artifacts such as [**Mana Vault**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/05e9fec4-1e0a-4206-ab2b-cc2543cba667?format=image) and [**Lotus Petal**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/6c877da3-68fa-41d0-8a24-8c79fcd8ecc1?format=image) to enable [**Tolarian Academy**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/ad7ac9a5-340f-4509-826c-7b9416d47887?format=image) to generate massive amounts of mana
- Cast [**Windfall**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/2aef4608-5ba8-4636-b5e7-cac57c5c0608?format=image) and [**Time Spiral**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/f3d62dbd-63db-4ac9-950f-9852627f23f2?format=image) to completely replenish the hand
- Use [**Mind Over Matter**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/6e091dd6-149f-46ea-bae0-224e79e3aacb?format=image) to discard the newly drawn cards, untap [**Tolarian Academy**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/ad7ac9a5-340f-4509-826c-7b9416d47887?format=image) and fuel an even larger pool of mana
- [**Stroke of Genius**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5e977755-8ea4-4a8b-90c4-dd175321e05d?format=image) as a draw spell and a mill-out weapon against the opponent
- [**Fireball**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/ad457b10-6e00-411f-b827-ff844f9b300d?format=image) is heavily utilized as insurance against "tower decks" (massive decks immune to mill) or situations where generating the full 60 mana for a mill-kill is impossible
- [**Pyroblast**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/15d2d7cd-d29d-4f19-8490-88bd526ff1c5?format=image) effective not only in mirror matches but also as a direct answer to hate cards like [**Arcane Laboratory**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/2f4a88e8-aab0-488a-a0b8-fa3feedbf278?format=image)
- [**Wasteland**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/99ff731b-8399-40c8-b539-ba6ba5783771?format=image) specifically chosen to disrupt opposing strategies in the mirror match
- Red splash in the main deck to gain an advantage in the mirror match, splashes White and Black additionally in the sideboard to further optimize and secure a favorable matchup against the same deck
- MoMa Winter
