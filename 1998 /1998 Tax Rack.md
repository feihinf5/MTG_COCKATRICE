Note
- Grand Prix-Rio de Janeiro 1998 winning deck by Jon Finkel
- Converts the massive land advantage from [**Land Tax**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/1301e203-7d9a-4735-8db3-7882ad70d343?format=image) by swapping those lands for fresh cards via [**Scroll Rack**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5f7346d8-1aef-4618-88e6-74bd8865e0f3?format=image)
- [**Tithe**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/aae08938-e563-4322-b2eb-db81913ea730?format=image) is often included for additional land searching
- Leverages the massive hand size to massively buff creatures with [**Empyrial Armor**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5518a79f-bcae-417a-b01b-b6ff572be0be?format=image) or discards the excess lands to fuel a board-wiping [**Firestorm**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e674aa8a-668a-4345-95ee-73a0b87bbcb1?format=image)
- [**Land Tax**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/1301e203-7d9a-4735-8db3-7882ad70d343?format=image) was banned in the Extended format on July 1998 https://mtg.fandom.com/wiki/Banned_and_restricted_cards/Timeline#1998
