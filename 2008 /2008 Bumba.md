Note
- Grand Prix-Rimini 2008 winning deck by Emanuele Giusti
- [**Wizened Cenn**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/585f1c8e-6898-4def-8e3f-d45cd263f776?format=image), [**Thistledown Liege**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/20e34233-0972-4aa7-a5ab-eabed2235148?format=image) and [**Wilt-Leaf Liege**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e6a2881f-e771-47d7-a39e-692054ee727f?format=image) pump up the team to quickly turn small creatures into massive threats
- [**Spectral Procession**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5e26bd0c-5ec7-4653-8e63-747157c20af1?format=image) and [**Cloudgoat Ranger**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9e344859-7248-4498-8303-252f196a007b?format=image) instantly flood the board with evasion-capable creatures
- [**Windbrisk Heights**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9df6a31a-5c49-4506-b8f8-84c9ab4a2ece?format=image) triggers easily due to the wide token board state, letting you cheat expensive spells like [**Cloudgoat Ranger**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9e344859-7248-4498-8303-252f196a007b?format=image) or [**Ajani Goldmane**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/2a1470a6-d09d-4a2a-84a6-d56e32ed237a?format=image) into play for just one white mana
- [**Figure of Destiny**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0da69523-cece-425a-b08a-fb27fac29374?format=image) serves as a scaling mana sink, while [**Ajani Goldmane**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/2a1470a6-d09d-4a2a-84a6-d56e32ed237a?format=image) and [**Mirrorweave**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/dea002c6-d15f-4536-9349-92c77ae5021a?format=image) (target [**Wizened Cenn**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/585f1c8e-6898-4def-8e3f-d45cd263f776?format=image)) provide game-ending anthems or lethal damage spikes
- [**Unmake**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/338f7852-9f69-4406-b6ad-93a2574296f7?format=image) provides instant-speed, unconditional exiling to clear key blockers or opposing threats
