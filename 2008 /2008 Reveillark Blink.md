Note
- 2008 Japan National Championship winning deck by Ooiso Masashi
- Cast [**Reveillark**] for its Evoke cost to triggers its leaves-the-battlefield ability and reanimate any two creatures with power 2 or less from your graveyard (e.g. [**Kitchen Finks**] + [**Mulldrifter**] or [**Venser, Shaper Savant**])
- Cast [**Momentary Blink**] targeting a creature with an Enter-the-Battlefield (ETB) ability, e.g. [**Mulldrifter**] to draw 2 cards. (If cast for Evoke, blinking it before its sacrifice trigger resolves keeps the 2/2 body and draws 2 more cards), e.g. [**Kitchen Finks**] to gain 2 life or blink it to remove its -1/-1 counter from Persist, e.g. [**Riftwing Cloudskate**] or [**Venser, Shaper Savant**] to return opposing threats or spells to their owner's hand
- By cycling [**Reveillark**] and [**Momentary Blink**], you can repeatedly return [**Venser, Shaper Savant**] or [**Riftwing Cloudskate**] to clear the opponent's board turn after turn, out-resourcing them under the protection of [**Cryptic Command**] and [**Rune Snag**]
