Note
- 2008 Japan National Championship winning deck by Ooiso Masashi
- Cast [**Reveillark**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5629edd4-b5d3-43f8-8c7c-f9d916e35158?format=image) for its Evoke cost to triggers its leaves-the-battlefield ability and reanimate any two creatures with power 2 or less from your graveyard (e.g. [**Kitchen Finks**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/7d203208-8a21-4c68-afa2-4efd8726f026?format=image) + [**Mulldrifter**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/a97cfefa-ade7-49f6-b2aa-1118b9db4935?format=image) or [**Venser, Shaper Savant**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0e84fc99-4045-4518-b588-512a675f2933?format=image))
- Cast [**Momentary Blink**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/032e072a-0630-472b-9106-5df554dff785?format=image) targeting a creature with an Enter-the-Battlefield (ETB) ability, e.g. [**Mulldrifter**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/a97cfefa-ade7-49f6-b2aa-1118b9db4935?format=image) to draw 2 cards. (If cast for Evoke, blinking it before its sacrifice trigger resolves keeps the 2/2 body and draws 2 more cards), e.g. [**Kitchen Finks**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/7d203208-8a21-4c68-afa2-4efd8726f026?format=image) to gain 2 life or blink it to remove its -1/-1 counter from Persist, e.g. [**Riftwing Cloudskate**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/78ad6c86-8cfb-4f24-b8e8-ecbeffc949b8?format=image) or [**Venser, Shaper Savant**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0e84fc99-4045-4518-b588-512a675f2933?format=image) to return opposing threats or spells to their owner's hand
- By cycling [**Reveillark**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5629edd4-b5d3-43f8-8c7c-f9d916e35158?format=image) and [**Momentary Blink**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/032e072a-0630-472b-9106-5df554dff785?format=image), you can repeatedly return [**Venser, Shaper Savant**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0e84fc99-4045-4518-b588-512a675f2933?format=image) or [**Riftwing Cloudskate**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/78ad6c86-8cfb-4f24-b8e8-ecbeffc949b8?format=image) to clear the opponent's board turn after turn, out-resourcing them under the protection of [**Cryptic Command**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/829e3d6e-5d7c-4cc4-a7a6-7cbf5a7442ba?format=image) and [**Rune Snag**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/45b6cadf-1974-47c8-98d8-ba413486c3b5?format=image)
