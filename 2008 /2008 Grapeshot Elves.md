Note
- Pro Tour-Berlin 2008 winning deck by Luis Scott-Vargas
- Setup at turn 1 or 2, play mana dorks ([**Llanowar Elves**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/913907e2-28dd-4080-b725-52a43868e060?format=image), [**Elves of Deep Shadow**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/7bea6dea-d767-4092-a285-305d9997357a?format=image), [**Heritage Druid**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/a726acbd-724e-46c5-a4cf-4aee7c2abb16?format=image))
- The Combo at turn 2 or 3, cast [**Glimpse of Nature**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/1ddcd76b-a7a1-4ae6-bf4a-f929c6574bdc?format=image) (or multiple copies), play cheap 1-mana Elves to keep drawing and untapping [**Nettle Sentinel**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/f9f21681-fd36-4106-8395-3153599a08a6?format=image), search out key missing combo pieces mid-turn using [**Summoner's Pact**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/948b026c-cfce-462b-afb6-7a383bd121de?format=image)
- Refilling the Hand with [**Weird Harvest**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/3cdfa8b3-393b-4bb6-9265-faa4ab7126d2?format=image) or [**Regal Force**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/674a694a-22b8-4fb8-b901-91d64564c0db?format=image), cast [**Weird Harvest**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/3cdfa8b3-393b-4bb6-9265-faa4ab7126d2?format=image) for a large X to search your library for X cheap 1-mana Elves, guaranteeing the combo doesn't stall
- Fetch [**Regal Force**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/674a694a-22b8-4fb8-b901-91d64564c0db?format=image) via [**Summoner's Pact**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/948b026c-cfce-462b-afb6-7a383bd121de?format=image) to instantly draw 10–20 cards if [**Glimpse of Nature**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/1ddcd76b-a7a1-4ae6-bf4a-f929c6574bdc?format=image) alone isn't enough
- [**Grapeshot**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4ee33cb6-768e-44a0-b6f4-b8638aa84330?format=image) once your Storm count (number of spells cast this turn) reaches 20+, convert 2 Elves into red mana via [**Birchlore Rangers**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/8ce3a3a1-3569-4909-a604-f78d4888781e?format=image) to deal lethal, unblockable damage directly to the opponent
