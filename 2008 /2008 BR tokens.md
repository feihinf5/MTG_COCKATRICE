Note
- 2008 Italy National Championship winning deck by William Cavaglieri
- Floods the board using cheap token generators ([**Bitterblossom**], [**Mogg War Marshal**], [**Marsh Flitter**], [**Kher Keep**])
- [**Torrent of Souls**] reanimates a creature while granting all your creatures +2/+0 and Haste to deal 15–20+ damage in a single turn
- [**Nantuko Husk**] consumes extra tokens for instant +2/+2 boosts per creature, turning wide boards into massive lethal attacks when paired with [**Torrent of Souls**]
