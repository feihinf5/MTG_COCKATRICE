Note
- 2008 Italy National Championship winning deck by William Cavaglieri
- Floods the board using cheap token generators ([**Bitterblossom**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/8145fed6-6b51-420a-84cf-4ea5e0aa1883?format=image), [**Mogg War Marshal**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/8b9e0bdb-b615-447a-b80d-d7244c25c56e?format=image), [**Marsh Flitter**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/040e1039-1943-4c2d-aa98-b7f9519de321?format=image), [**Kher Keep**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/dde3b332-5bc3-47bc-ba24-5437f04c21a0?format=image))
- [**Torrent of Souls**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4d1e3eff-d6b2-4758-ac50-91f79cd21b93?format=image) reanimates a creature while granting all your creatures +2/+0 and Haste to deal 15–20+ damage in a single turn
- [**Nantuko Husk**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/7a8f8f83-65bb-4da3-8263-e58162ebb24b?format=image) consumes extra tokens for instant +2/+2 boosts per creature, turning wide boards into massive lethal attacks when paired with [**Torrent of Souls**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4d1e3eff-d6b2-4758-ac50-91f79cd21b93?format=image)
