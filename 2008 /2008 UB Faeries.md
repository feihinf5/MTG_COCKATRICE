Note
- World Championships 2008 winning deck by Antti Malin
- [**Bitterblossom**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/8145fed6-6b51-420a-84cf-4ea5e0aa1883?format=image) creates a 1/1 flying Faerie Rogue token every turn at the cost of 1 life
- [**Spellstutter Sprite**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4e5ba4a9-a282-4d4b-b25a-179e05e458f4?format=image) acts as a counterspell attached to a 1/1 flying body with Flash and paired with [**Bitterblossom**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/8145fed6-6b51-420a-84cf-4ea5e0aa1883?format=image), it frequently counters 2-, 3-, and 4-mana spells for just 2 mana
- Casting [**Mistbind Clique**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/cfc421e2-0dd4-4bdf-b5f5-a60c4b0df63b?format=image) during the opponent's Upkeep phase effectively skips their turn
- [**Thoughtseize**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/3df8c148-e87d-4043-9d8b-ec72bf8b6d5d?format=image) strips away the opponent's primary answer or sweeper on Turn 1
- [**Vendilion Clique**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/f53d8540-fb6d-4d4c-b467-ebfbfa53c880?format=image) cast at Flash speed (often during the opponent's draw step) to strip their best card while adding a 3/1 flying clock
- [**Mutavault**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/8ca3c48b-f104-4292-9a4e-2ce87a65893c?format=image) and [**Faerie Conclave**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/69df4707-09c5-48e7-9681-0cf7960aeeb1?format=image) turn into creatures and [**Mutavault**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/8ca3c48b-f104-4292-9a4e-2ce87a65893c?format=image) counts as a Faerie, buffing [**Spellstutter Sprite**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4e5ba4a9-a282-4d4b-b25a-179e05e458f4?format=image) count or acting as champion-bait for [**Mistbind Clique**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/cfc421e2-0dd4-4bdf-b5f5-a60c4b0df63b?format=image)
- [**Cryptic Command**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/829e3d6e-5d7c-4cc4-a7a6-7cbf5a7442ba?format=image) and [**Agony Warp**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e246294f-7614-48e9-a655-647faf0bf697?format=image) are highly flexible instant-speed interaction that handles any board state or clears opposing blockers
- [**Sower of Temptation**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/2f5320da-7214-4348-84d8-74bf951c9f2f?format=image) to steals the opponent's biggest threat while maintaining air superiority
