Note
- 2008 Canadian National Championship winning deck by Dan Lanthier
- Brings back two creatures with power 2 or less from the graveyard when it leaves the battlefield ([**Mulldrifter**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/a97cfefa-ade7-49f6-b2aa-1118b9db4935?format=image), [**Venser, Shaper Savant**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0e84fc99-4045-4518-b588-512a675f2933?format=image), [**Body Double**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/d66895b2-07f9-46b1-80e6-2d573e532fc5?format=image), [**Sower of Temptation**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/2f5320da-7214-4348-84d8-74bf951c9f2f?format=image))
- Infinite Loop Setup that requires [**Greater Gargadon**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/653ddfa0-2088-4503-a3ab-b0f1d55d8351?format=image) (suspended as a 0-mana sacrifice outlet), [**Reveillark**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5629edd4-b5d3-43f8-8c7c-f9d916e35158?format=image) and [**Body Double**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/d66895b2-07f9-46b1-80e6-2d573e532fc5?format=image) in the graveyard
- [**Body Double**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/d66895b2-07f9-46b1-80e6-2d573e532fc5?format=image) enters copying [**Reveillark**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5629edd4-b5d3-43f8-8c7c-f9d916e35158?format=image) then sacrifice [**Body Double**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/d66895b2-07f9-46b1-80e6-2d573e532fc5?format=image) to [**Greater Gargadon**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/653ddfa0-2088-4503-a3ab-b0f1d55d8351?format=image), next [**Body Double**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/d66895b2-07f9-46b1-80e6-2d573e532fc5?format=image)'s leaves-the-battlefield trigger returns itself and another power equal or less than 2 creature, repeat infinitely to bounce all opposing lands/permanents (via [**Venser, Shaper Savant**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0e84fc99-4045-4518-b588-512a675f2933?format=image)), draw the entire deck (via [**Reveillark**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5629edd4-b5d3-43f8-8c7c-f9d916e35158?format=image)), gain infinite life (via [**Aven Riftwatcher**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/f6a4394a-9ce2-4876-8a04-38e3775123af?format=image)), or steal all opposing creatures (via [**Sower of Temptation**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/2f5320da-7214-4348-84d8-74bf951c9f2f?format=image))
- Uses [**Momentary Blink**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/032e072a-0630-472b-9106-5df554dff785?format=image) to reset Evoked creatures, dodge removal or re-trigger ETB effects
- Uses [**Wrath of God**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b969f680-b176-4bfa-a160-714de8e03c25?format=image) for board clears (triggering [**Reveillark**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5629edd4-b5d3-43f8-8c7c-f9d916e35158?format=image)), backed by [**Rune Snag**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/45b6cadf-1974-47c8-98d8-ba413486c3b5?format=image) and [**Pact of Negation**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/cca467a2-a2b3-4bdf-9d60-62979f675347?format=image) to protect the combo
