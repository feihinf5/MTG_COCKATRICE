Note
- Grand Prix-Vancouver 2008 winning deck by Paul Cheon
- [**Tarmogoyf**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b6876d9e-0908-43ac-8542-09c7aa02b5ba?format=image) provides an incredible, cheap combat body (often an early 4/5 or 5/6) for just 2 mana
- [**Vedalken Shackles**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/cef06f56-a7a8-4c0f-8755-56b46d2aa82c?format=image) & [**Threads of Disloyalty**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/999440a5-9659-4218-8594-1950f1155975?format=image) to steal the opponent's creatures, since the land base heavily favors [**Snow-Covered Island**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/6abf0692-07d1-4b72-af06-93d0e338589d?format=image), [**Vedalken Shackles**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/cef06f56-a7a8-4c0f-8755-56b46d2aa82c?format=image) repeatedly steals high-value threats, while [**Threads of Disloyalty**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/999440a5-9659-4218-8594-1950f1155975?format=image) permanently steals 1- and 2-mana creatures
- [**Rude Awakening**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0164e2ed-b722-4a7d-a4fb-5401af008cbf?format=image) primary finisher in late-game stalled boards with Entwine, it untaps all lands and turns them into 2/2 creatures with haste for an instant, un-blockable lethal swing
- [**Sensei’s Divining Top**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4a08ca06-58db-4ce6-b490-be4bea8956a1?format=image) + Fetch Lands to provides continuous hand filtering, combined with 8 fetch lands, you can look at 3 cards, shuffle away unwanted cards with a fetch land and draw what you need
- [**Miren, the Moaning Well**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/53d414f0-15ae-446b-a8d7-56c1b502740c.?format=image) allows you to sacrifice stolen creatures (via [**Vedalken Shackles**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/cef06f56-a7a8-4c0f-8755-56b46d2aa82c?format=image) or [**Threads of Disloyalty**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/999440a5-9659-4218-8594-1950f1155975?format=image)) to gain life, preventing the opponent from ever getting them back
- [**Academy Ruins**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/af09af65-0a3b-42df-b0fd-372e2158beac?format=image) loops destroyed artifacts back to the top of your library
