Note
- 2008 Australia National Championships winning deck by Aaron Nicastri
- Target your own [**Swans of Bryn Argoll**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0cdf78b7-3c32-422a-a73f-d198e6f06290?format=image) with [**Seismic Assault**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/14363cc7-ef76-41ad-9307-8ab3ed469a23?format=image) by discarding a land card, because [**Swans of Bryn Argoll**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0cdf78b7-3c32-422a-a73f-d198e6f06290?format=image) converts damage into card draws, each discarded land draws 2 cards, exponentially growing your hand size
- Repeat this loop to draw your entire library then throw all accumulated land cards directly at the opponent for lethal damage
- [**Beseech the Queen**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/64ee0a93-0f6d-42be-bdca-1de5422d8d54?format=image) to fetch combo pieces and hand disruption like [**Thoughtseize**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/3df8c148-e87d-4043-9d8b-ec72bf8b6d5d?format=image) to proactively clear away opposing interaction
- Including [**Dakmor Salvage**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/8f7f06e8-d8ee-435c-965c-8e1302a8ec10?format=image) allows you to Dredge instead of drawing cards during the combo, safely self-milling your library
- [**Gaea's Blessing**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/2f5ded38-db2d-490c-9521-200da0386232?format=image) is included to trigger when milled by [**Dakmor Salvage**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/8f7f06e8-d8ee-435c-965c-8e1302a8ec10?format=image), shuffling your graveyard back into your library to prevent accidentally losing to self-mill (deckout)
