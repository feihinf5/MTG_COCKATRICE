Note
- The Finals07 winning deck by Fujita Osamu
- Rotates the hand using looters like [**Merfolk Looter**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/28e4e99d-5834-4f6c-b915-4f95edc1337f?format=image) to dump big threats ([**Akroma, Angel of Wrath**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/96c9646b-9223-4d4f-a7d0-fc6208cac8b8?format=image), [**Bogardan Hellkite**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5852f528-92aa-45cf-8436-5774691676d3?format=image)) into the graveyard, reanimating them with [**Dread Return**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/d7e304fc-0ace-459e-8d2f-376f1899639c?format=image)
- Uses [**Makeshift Mannequin**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/79029161-a2c9-4cf6-94c0-31400daa0e8e?format=image) to recur [**Bogardan Hellkite**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5852f528-92aa-45cf-8436-5774691676d3?format=image) for repeated damage
- Applies early beatdown pressure using efficient discard outlets/enablers like [**Oona's Prowler**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9675b8ea-47f9-440e-9535-de879da53f76?format=image) and [**Vexing Sphinx**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/81cc1248-85c8-428f-ba08-96d188167eaa?format=image)
- Uses [**Thoughtseize**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/3df8c148-e87d-4043-9d8b-ec72bf8b6d5d?format=image) to strip opposing counterspells and graveyard hate from the opponent's hand
