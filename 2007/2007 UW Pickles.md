Note
- Grand Prix-Krakow 2007 winning deck by Paul Cheon
- Features the devastating hard-lock combo utilizing [**Vesuvan Shapeshifter**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/458031cc-238d-45f0-9a81-b52e82286e0f?format=image) and [**Brine Elemental**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/8dab2c73-501b-46c9-bc76-134b50d35bb8?format=image)
- Incorporates [**Ancestral Vision**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/bccedc4d-38c7-4bf3-9ca7-4febd6c49d3d?format=image) as a powerful draw engine that provides massive card advantage
- Adopts [**Cryptic Command**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/829e3d6e-5d7c-4cc4-a7a6-7cbf5a7442ba?format=image) as a highly flexible, versatile modal counterspell that answers almost any situation
