Note
- Grand Prix-Columbus 2007 winning deck by Steven Sadin
- Uses [**Flash**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/63af3c26-5b1f-46f6-9aa2-036c615bf5ea?format=image) to cheat [**Protean Hulk**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/3d978332-95bf-4f86-9e67-06f10983c267?format=image) into play and immediately sacrifice it to trigger its death ability, chaining together creatures to win instantly
- [**Protean Hulk**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/3d978332-95bf-4f86-9e67-06f10983c267?format=image)'s death fetches [**Karmic Guide**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/77d23045-905b-44cb-9af9-cc6ad717477d?format=image) and [**Carrion Feeder**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/88042031-64af-4f84-85d5-95992b43aa6c?format=image)
- [**Karmic Guide**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/77d23045-905b-44cb-9af9-cc6ad717477d?format=image)'s ETB (enters-the-battlefield) ability returns [**Protean Hulk**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/3d978332-95bf-4f86-9e67-06f10983c267?format=image) to the battlefield, which is then sacrificed to [**Carrion Feeder**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/88042031-64af-4f84-85d5-95992b43aa6c?format=image) to fetch [**Kiki-Jiki, Mirror Breaker**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/162018eb-5483-4fa2-9c5a-abb639eecf91?format=image)
- [**Kiki-Jiki, Mirror Breaker**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/162018eb-5483-4fa2-9c5a-abb639eecf91?format=image) copies [**Karmic Guide**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/77d23045-905b-44cb-9af9-cc6ad717477d?format=image) and in response to the copy ability, [**Kiki-Jiki, Mirror Breaker**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/162018eb-5483-4fa2-9c5a-abb639eecf91?format=image) is sacrificed to [**Carrion Feeder**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/88042031-64af-4f84-85d5-95992b43aa6c?format=image)
- The [**Karmic Guide**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/77d23045-905b-44cb-9af9-cc6ad717477d?format=image) copy's ETB ability reanimates [**Kiki-Jiki, Mirror Breaker**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/162018eb-5483-4fa2-9c5a-abb639eecf91?format=image)
- Repeating this loop generates an infinite army of [**Karmic Guide**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/77d23045-905b-44cb-9af9-cc6ad717477d?format=image) tokens with haste to swing for the game-winning attack
- Includes [**Body Snatcher**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/c7d4c858-5a11-485d-a514-12a6d80459f0?format=image) to recover key combo pieces if they are accidentally drawn into the hand
- Maximized by the legality of a full 4 copies of [**Mystical Tutor**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5d98101f-e32a-4a4a-a649-faa920d111ee?format=image) in Legacy at the time
- Integrates the [**Counterbalance**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/c329ff2b-0331-4934-a8df-870dd7bf402b?format=image) + [**Sensei's Divining Top**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4a08ca06-58db-4ce6-b490-be4bea8956a1?format=image) engine to secure the combo which also naturally synergizes with [**Dark Confidant**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/94f7a441-bf2d-46fb-a7b6-9bd6137f86d9?format=image)
- [**Flash**] was banned during June 2007 https://web.archive.org/web/20210502202147/http://www.wizards.com/default.asp?x=dci/announce/dci20070601a
