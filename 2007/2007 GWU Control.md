Note
- 2007 United States National Championship winning deck by Luis Scott-Vargas
- [**Chord of Calling**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e064174b-8f07-4fea-9eef-c3b5d0220b1a?format=image) can search up the exact creature directly onto the battlefield, e.g. fetch [**Mystic Snake**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5f5c59dd-e3e7-4179-b10b-d066e870eafb?format=image) to counter a crucial spell or fetch [**Venser, Shaper Savant**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0e84fc99-4045-4518-b588-512a675f2933?format=image) or [**Willbender**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/25f2a1d8-8b47-4278-afe1-3f3ede60fed9?format=image) to disrupt an opponent's targeting or permanent
- "Pickles" lock which completely prevents opponent from untapping their cards for the rest of the game, [**Brine Elemental**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/8dab2c73-501b-46c9-bc76-134b50d35bb8?format=image) and [**Vesuvan Shapeshifter**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/458031cc-238d-45f0-9a81-b52e82286e0f?format=image) on the battlefield
- Turn [**Vesuvan Shapeshifter**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/458031cc-238d-45f0-9a81-b52e82286e0f?format=image) face-down during upkeep for its morph cost
- Flip [**Vesuvan Shapeshifter**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/458031cc-238d-45f0-9a81-b52e82286e0f?format=image) face-up for its cheap Morph cost, choosing to copy the [**Brine Elemental**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/8dab2c73-501b-46c9-bc76-134b50d35bb8?format=image) and this triggers the copied face-up ability: "Opponents skip their next untap step."
- Repeat this every single turn and opponent will never get an untap step again, locking down their lands and blockers permanently
- Alternatively can overwhelm the opponent with the card draw from [**Arcanis the Omnipotent**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0ed53483-4bec-491b-9ae6-afc6faf122a2?format=image), the stat-boosting/reducing static ability of [**Crovax, Ascendant Hero**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/63049fe4-de25-4606-a271-4ccb1b8298f1?format=image) or beats from [**Loxodon Hierarch**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/a1d3ccca-8040-4a5f-9f2b-6fdb1a033234?format=image)
