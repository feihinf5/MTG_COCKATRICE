Note
- 2007 Argentina National Championship winning deck by Nicolas Kohan
- Cast [**Smallpox**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/175d5a88-2597-4e85-aed6-7a65c0595fb4?format=image) and you can discard [**Cry of Contrition**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/62016f24-3f40-406b-8e29-30a6545d0fc1?format=image) which has Haunt and when the creature it haunts dies (perhaps to the same Smallpox or an combat trade), the opponent discards another card
- Deploy [**Dark Confidant**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/94f7a441-bf2d-46fb-a7b6-9bd6137f86d9?format=image) to keep your hand fully loaded with removal and discard spells and [**Tarmogoyf**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b6876d9e-0908-43ac-8542-09c7aa02b5ba?format=image) will rapidly grow into a massive 2-mana 4/5 or 5/6
- Use [**Putrefy**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0a16086c-5a74-45d0-8b38-e832cfbc80f7?format=image) to cleanly destroy any problematic creatures or artifacts that slipped through your discard net and [**Slaughter Pact**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/42696fdb-de1f-44ae-bef3-b6af068958d0?format=image) as a 0-mana emergency button to kill a threat while you are fully tapped out.
- With the opponent completely locked at zero cards, [**The Rack**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/696d1e25-5c25-4522-b085-90d49fe23a18?format=image) will consistently tick for 3 damage every single turn.
- Activate [**Treetop Village**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/de15f8fd-499c-4fa0-9a10-425f81c61579?format=image) to close out the remaining points of life
