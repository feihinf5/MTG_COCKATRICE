Note
- 2007 Japan National Championship winning deck by Kitayama Masaya
- cast [**Cry of Contrition**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/62016f24-3f40-406b-8e29-30a6545d0fc1?format=image) targeting your opponent and When it resolves, it forces a discard and then haunts one of your own creatures (like [**Augur of Skulls**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/945404d5-9e44-45e7-84fd-5f8c893e7d27?format=image), when you sacrifice that haunted creature to [**Smallpox**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/175d5a88-2597-4e85-aed6-7a65c0595fb4?format=image) or [**Augur of Skulls**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/945404d5-9e44-45e7-84fd-5f8c893e7d27?format=image), the Haunt triggers from exile, forcing your opponent to discard a second time
- Deploy [**Dark Confidant**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/94f7a441-bf2d-46fb-a7b6-9bd6137f86d9?format=image) to keep your hand fully loaded with removal and discard spells and [**Tarmogoyf**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b6876d9e-0908-43ac-8542-09c7aa02b5ba?format=image) will rapidly grow into a massive 2-mana 4/5 or 5/6
- With the opponent completely locked at zero cards, [**The Rack**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/696d1e25-5c25-4522-b085-90d49fe23a18?format=image) will consistently tick for 3 damage every single turn.
- Activate [**Treetop Village**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/de15f8fd-499c-4fa0-9a10-425f81c61579?format=image) to close out the remaining points of life
