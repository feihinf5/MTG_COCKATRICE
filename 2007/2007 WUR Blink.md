Note
- 2007 Canadian National Championship winning deck by Andrew Ting-A-Kee
- Cast [**Stone Rain**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/d2334c10-fa96-4f8e-8187-c7ecc00cbac8?format=image) to destroy opponentkey lands and if can land an early [**Grand Arbiter Augustin IV**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/a2ac328b-923f-48dd-a4f5-de389ade9125?format=image), spells become cheaper while their spells cost 1 more, amplifying the land destruction
- Use [**Momentary Blink**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/032e072a-0630-472b-9106-5df554dff785?format=image) on [**Court Hussar**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4970f492-d4fc-4833-a083-05e422a3c456?format=image) to dig deep for answers without paying its mana cost again
- Use [**Momentary Blink**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/032e072a-0630-472b-9106-5df554dff785?format=image) on [**Riftwing Cloudskate**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/78ad6c86-8cfb-4f24-b8e8-ecbeffc949b8?format=image) or [**Venser, Shaper Savant**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0e84fc99-4045-4518-b588-512a675f2933?format=image) to repeatedly bounce the opponent's lands or threats back to their hand
