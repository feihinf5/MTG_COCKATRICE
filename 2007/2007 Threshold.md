Note
- 2007 Legacy World Championships winning deck by Peter Olszewski
- Combine [**Wasteland**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/99ff731b-8399-40c8-b539-ba6ba5783771?format=image) and [**Stifle**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/2d7643c0-b2db-478f-944e-b27b77bad3eb?format=image) to completely devastate opponent's mana base
- Use [**Stifle**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/2d7643c0-b2db-478f-944e-b27b77bad3eb?format=image) to counter their fetch land activations, effectively destroying their land for just 1 blue mana
- Turn on Threshold and [**Nimble Mongoose**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/99e5ecf5-a662-4df0-a6ba-9177c62b6503?format=image) balloons into a massive, un-targetable 3/3, [**Werebear**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/964cf7e3-932d-432f-8ad4-9bd651aada96?format=image) jumps from a 1/1 mana dork to a massive 4/4 attacker, drop [**Tarmogoyf**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b6876d9e-0908-43ac-8542-09c7aa02b5ba?format=image) easily becomes a 2-mana 4/5 or 5/6
- Deny opponent using [**Daze**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/d03bff25-0d5e-4dcf-8d75-6df846afea3b?format=image) and [**Force of Will**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9a879b60-4381-447d-8a5a-8e0b6a1d49ca?format=image) and use [**Spell Snare**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/35554fdf-c70a-4baa-a35a-414caa9978be?format=image) to cleanly answer critical 2-mana defensive spells or opposing [**Tarmogoyf**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b6876d9e-0908-43ac-8542-09c7aa02b5ba?format=image)
- Use [**Snapback**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/de9135ba-9b6a-4443-a1c4-882f9bfae626?format=image) (cast for free by pitching a blue card) or [**Rushing River**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/52ddf7bf-de9c-4657-8d5b-79869d36fa63?format=image) (kicked by sacrificing a land) to bounce problematic threat away at the end of their turn, clearing the path for a lethal swing
- Use [**Predict**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/04dc842c-3250-44ed-906c-38f14bf1f0e2?format=image) in combination with [**Portent**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e040be83-3fb5-4da5-ba7a-4923b8854b74?format=image) or [**Brainstorm**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/8d42d7aa-7f53-4cfc-842a-086aab2448d1?format=image), if you know the top card of your library (or your opponent's), [**Predict**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/04dc842c-3250-44ed-906c-38f14bf1f0e2?format=image) names that card, puts it in the graveyard (helping Threshold), and draws you 2 cards for just 2 mana
- The "Counter-Top" Shift against aggressive decks or combo decks where your land destruction plan isn't enough, your sideboard allows you to transition into a hard control engine by bring in [**Counterbalance**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/c329ff2b-0331-4934-a8df-870dd7bf402b?format=image) and [**Sensei's Divining Top**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4a08ca06-58db-4ce6-b490-be4bea8956a1?format=image) to let you look at the top 3 cards of your library at any time, rearranging them to match the exact mana value of whatever spell your opponent tries to cast, countering it for 0 mana
- Bring in [**Umezawa's Jitte**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/3b6e5956-f795-451b-bb24-56462d1ced27?format=image) to easily pick off small opposing creatures or gain chunks of life to race aggressive decks
