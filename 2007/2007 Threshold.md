Note
- 2007 Legacy World Championships winning deck by Peter Olszewski
- Combine [**Wasteland**] and [**Stifle**] to completely devastate opponent's mana base
- Use [**Stifle**] to counter their fetch land activations (e.g., [**Polluted Delta**]), effectively destroying their land for just 1 blue mana
- Turn on Threshold and [**Nimble Mongoose**] balloons into a massive, un-targetable 3/3, [**Werebear**] jumps from a 1/1 mana dork to a massive 4/4 attacker, drop [**Tarmogoyf**] easily becomes a 2-mana 4/5 or 5/6
- Deny opponent using [**Daze**] and [**Force of Will**] and use [**Spell Snare**] to cleanly answer critical 2-mana defensive spells or opposing [**Tarmogoyfs**]
- Use [**Snapback**] (cast for free by pitching a blue card) or [**Rushing River**] (kicked by sacrificing a land) to bounce problematic prison piece (like [**Ensnaring Bridge**]) away at the end of their turn, clearing the path for a lethal swing
- Use [**Predict**] in combination with [**Portent**] or [**Brainstorm**], if you know the top card of your library (or your opponent's), [**Predict**] names that card, puts it in the graveyard (helping Threshold), and draws you 2 cards for just 2 mana
- The "Counter-Top" Shift against aggressive decks or combo decks where your land destruction plan isn't enough, your sideboard allows you to transition into a hard control engine by bring in [**Counterbalance**] and [**Sensei's Divining Top**] to let you look at the top 3 cards of your library at any time, rearranging them to match the exact mana value of whatever spell your opponent tries to cast, countering it for 0 mana
- Bring in [**Umezawa's Jitte**] to easily pick off small opposing creatures or gain chunks of life to race aggressive decks
