Note
- World Championships 2007 winning deck by Uri Peleg
- Turn 1 [**Thoughtseize**] is the best opening play
- Premier Threat is [**Tarmogoyf**]
- Deploy [**Doran, the Siege Tower**] becomes a massive 3-mana 5/5 while [**Birds of Paradise**] transforms into a 1/1 flying attacker or attack with [**Ohran Viper**] which draws you cards when it connects with under [**Doran, the Siege Tower**], it hits as a 3/3 instead of a 1/3
- [**Nameless Inversion**] and [**Eyeblight's Ending**] deal with opposing creatures with [**Nameless Inversion**] is especially notable because it has the "Tribal" card type, which pumps your [**Tarmogoyf**] even further by adding a unique card type to the graveyard
- [**Profane Command**] can simultaneously bring back a dead [**Tarmogoyf**] or [**Doran, the Siege Tower**] to the battlefield while killing an opposing threat, draining the opponent’s life total, or giving your team fear to swing for the win
