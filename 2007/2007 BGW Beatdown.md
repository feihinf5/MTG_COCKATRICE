Note
- World Championships 2007 winning deck by Uri Peleg
- Turn 1 [**Thoughtseize**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/3df8c148-e87d-4043-9d8b-ec72bf8b6d5d?format=image) is the best opening play
- Premier Threat is [**Tarmogoyf**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b6876d9e-0908-43ac-8542-09c7aa02b5ba?format=image)
- Deploy [**Doran, the Siege Tower**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b006b169-295d-4ead-8e8e-29a9c3246025?format=image) becomes a massive 3-mana 5/5 while [**Birds of Paradise**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/44060205-8a5c-4f6d-8190-e65d5250935a?format=image) transforms into a 1/1 flying attacker or attack with [**Ohran Viper**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/bd5fd0ee-2350-4eb0-8417-700459879f74?format=image) which draws you cards when it connects with under [**Doran, the Siege Tower**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b006b169-295d-4ead-8e8e-29a9c3246025?format=image), it hits as a 3/3 instead of a 1/3
- [**Nameless Inversion**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/94b4e4d2-2358-48d2-9a2a-3d17afea28f5?format=image) and [**Eyeblight's Ending**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e0c08701-7038-4d6b-bbf8-056fd8ffb226?format=image) deal with opposing creatures with [**Nameless Inversion**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/94b4e4d2-2358-48d2-9a2a-3d17afea28f5?format=image) is especially notable because it has the "Tribal" card type, which pumps your [**Tarmogoyf**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b6876d9e-0908-43ac-8542-09c7aa02b5ba?format=image) even further by adding a unique card type to the graveyard
- [**Profane Command**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/752bf7fd-c49e-42fa-bbdd-8ca5d4c89b2b?format=image) can simultaneously bring back a dead [**Tarmogoyf**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b6876d9e-0908-43ac-8542-09c7aa02b5ba?format=image) or [**Doran, the Siege Tower**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b006b169-295d-4ead-8e8e-29a9c3246025?format=image) to the battlefield while killing an opposing threat, draining the opponent’s life total, or giving your team fear to swing for the win
