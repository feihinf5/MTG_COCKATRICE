Note
- 2007 German National Championship winning deck by Bodo Rosner
- Use [**Wrath of God**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b969f680-b176-4bfa-a160-714de8e03c25?format=image) to reset the board
- [**Lightning Angel**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/322d5ba3-41cc-4e60-9d0a-074f48a71ea3?format=image) to chunks away at the opponent's life total while remaining untapped to block any incoming attackers
- Cast [**Compulsive Research**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/20b9c057-b80a-4e4e-a0d7-34b7ff3a69f4?format=image) to draw three cards, discarding excess lands or a [**Firemane Angel**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/493730ab-c30d-4830-a188-0462426fc5bf?format=image) and cast it from graveyard when mana is available
- Suspend [**Aeon Chronicler**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/2fbc2dd2-976b-4239-bef9-ea0f06be79ba?format=image) and could act as finisher
- [**Demonfire**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/af2ad333-722e-4d7e-972a-903c24068931?format=image) could act as uncounterable finisher
