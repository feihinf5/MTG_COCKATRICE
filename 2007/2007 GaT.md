Note
- 2007 Vintage Championship winning deck by Stephen Menendian
- Deploys [**Quirion Dryad**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/f6841ae6-b15f-488e-9cae-2cc5ec668278?format=image), digging deep into the library with cheap draw spells to pump its stats out of nowhere and attack
- Fills the graveyard and builds a massive hand size, pumping [**Psychatog**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/6757bf0e-489f-4be2-9e41-463b59f00dd1?format=image) for an explosive, instant-speed kill
- Whichever plan is executed, fetching [**Berserk**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e173c8ce-2352-405e-ad00-e3bb94ced1ad?format=image) via [**Cunning Wish**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/ca097675-5e82-493d-beab-9fc11efd7492?format=image) easily deals 20+ points of lethal damage out of nowhere
- [**Gush**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e755bbef-bf34-49c0-ae72-d70e3599de52?format=image) exponentially accelerates the deck's offensive power by the interaction with [**Fastbond**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/a575a9af-e1de-4a1d-91d8-440585377e4f?format=image) is devastating—bouncing tapped [**Islands**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/90a57c0e-fa61-45ef-955d-d296403967d5?format=image) to your hand lets you immediately replay them, creating a massive mana-acceleration loop
- [**Gush**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e755bbef-bf34-49c0-ae72-d70e3599de52?format=image) was retricted in Vintage format since June 2008 https://web.archive.org/web/20210429115057/http://www.wizards.com/default.asp?x=dci/announce/dci20080602a
