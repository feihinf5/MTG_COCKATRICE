Note
- 2007 Vintage Championship winning deck by Stephen Menendian
- Deploys [**Quirion Dryad**], digging deep into the library with cheap draw spells to pump its stats out of nowhere and attack
- Fills the graveyard and builds a massive hand size, pumping [**Psychatog**] for an explosive, instant-speed kill
- Whichever plan is executed, fetching [**Berserk**] via [**Cunning Wish**] easily deals 20+ points of lethal damage out of nowhere
- [**Gush**] exponentially accelerates the deck's offensive power by the interaction with [**Fastbond**] is devastating—bouncing tapped [**Islands**] to your hand lets you immediately replay them, creating a massive mana-acceleration loop
- [**Gush**] was retricted in Vintage format since June 2008 https://web.archive.org/web/20210429115057/http://www.wizards.com/default.asp?x=dci/announce/dci20080602a
