Note
- 2007 Great Britain National Championships winning deck by Craig Jones
- [**Troll Ascetic**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/d447186e-62a7-4767-bb85-6439fd795350?format=image) is incredibly difficult for control decks to deal with due to Hexproof and Regeneration while [**Tarmogoyf**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b6876d9e-0908-43ac-8542-09c7aa02b5ba?format=image) grows massive quickly to varied card types (Artifacts from [**Seal of Fire**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/82ff3740-93e2-454a-8e6b-e29d0c9d0fc2?format=image), Sorceries from [**Rift Bolt**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/88dde96e-6824-4d26-9fb5-86b9f3c50959?format=image) and Lands from [**Horizon Canopy**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/d5dfc25d-a17b-4ead-9484-e8a18b8fa176?format=image) or [**Gemstone Mine**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/26794aa3-7532-4611-a591-51a51e5f9189?format=image) etc.)
- Sacrifice [**Keldon Marauders**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/677c28db-34d2-4325-99d1-9b689753802f?format=image) right before its Vanishing trigger or sacrifice a land targeted by opponent removal to remove a time counter from [**Greater Gargadon**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/653ddfa0-2088-4503-a3ab-b0f1d55d8351?format=image)
- [**Treetop Village**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/de15f8fd-499c-4fa0-9a10-425f81c61579?format=image) against board clears like [**Damnation**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/26c68473-70ca-40ba-b5c6-71ec30f88a2c?format=image)
