Note
- 2007 Great Britain National Championships winning deck by Craig Jones
- [**Troll Ascetic**] is incredibly difficult for control decks to deal with due to Hexproof and Regeneration while [**Tarmogoyf**] grows massive quickly to varied card types (Artifacts from [**Seal of Fire**], Sorceries from [**Rift Bolt**] and Lands from [**Horizon Canopy**] or [**Gemstone Mine**] etc.)
- Sacrifice [**Keldon Marauders**] right before its Vanishing trigger or sacrifice a land targeted by opponent removal to remove a time counter from [**Greater Gargadon**]
- [**Treetop Village**] against board clears like [**Damnation**]
