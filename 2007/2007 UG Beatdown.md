Note
- Grand Prix-Florence 2007 winning deck by Kaneko Masami
- Deploys highly efficient creatures like [**Tarmogoyf**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b6876d9e-0908-43ac-8542-09c7aa02b5ba?format=image) and [**Riftwing Cloudskate**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/78ad6c86-8cfb-4f24-b8e8-ecbeffc949b8?format=image) to generate massive tempo advantage, beating down the opponent while backing them up with counterspells like [**Delay**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e821d337-4bc5-4401-ac9b-34adf4012b73?format=image)
- Utilizes [**Looter il-Kor**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/368ee06f-9021-4b65-9f53-9c326bf3a27f?format=image) to rapidly feed the graveyard and grow Tarmogoyf, giving the deck exceptionally high consistency
- Seamlessly pivots against control decks post-board by bringing in the "Pickles" combo, [**Brine Elemental**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/8dab2c73-501b-46c9-bc76-134b50d35bb8?format=image) + [**Vesuvan Shapeshifter**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/458031cc-238d-45f0-9a81-b52e82286e0f?format=image)
