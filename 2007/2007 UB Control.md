Note
- Pro Tour-Yokohama 2007 winning deck by Guillaume Wafo-Tapa
- Highly relies on [**Damnation**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/26c68473-70ca-40ba-b5c6-71ec30f88a2c?format=image) alongside [**Teferi, Mage of Zhalfir**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/3c0145f6-4e27-49e7-9ef6-bac6fa3de26d?format=image), who provides a massive advantage against control decks
- Focuses on accelerating mana using storage lands and [**Prismatic Lens**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/50f058a7-c3c7-4bdf-a66c-a2636a8bd9db?format=image) while controlling the game through removal and counterspells
- Uniquely runs a full 4 copies of [**Urborg, Tomb of Yawgmoth**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/19e1224f-82cb-4f41-8739-f880cba61bbb?format=image) exponentially increases the power and life-gain efficiency of [**Tendrils of Corruption**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/7f61db9e-ef88-4dc8-b90c-1f8b2d7e9bb9?format=image), smoothly stabilizes the deck's overall mana base
