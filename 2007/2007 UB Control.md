Note
- Pro Tour-Yokohama 2007 winning deck by Guillaume Wafo-Tapa
- Highly relies on [**Damnation**] alongside [**Teferi, Mage of Zhalfir**], who provides a massive advantage against control decks
- Focuses on accelerating mana using storage lands and [**Prismatic Lens**] while controlling the game through removal and counterspells
- Uniquely runs a full 4 copies of [**Urborg, Tomb of Yawgmoth**] exponentially increases the power and life-gain efficiency of [**Tendrils of Corruption**], smoothly stabilizes the deck's overall mana base
