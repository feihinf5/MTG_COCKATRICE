Note
- Pro Tour-Valencia 2007 winning deck by Remi Fortier
- Soft-lock via the [**Counterbalance**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/c329ff2b-0331-4934-a8df-870dd7bf402b?format=image) + [**Sensei's Divining Top**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4a08ca06-58db-4ce6-b490-be4bea8956a1?format=image) synergy while generating raw card advantage with [**Dark Confidant**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/94f7a441-bf2d-46fb-a7b6-9bd6137f86d9?format=image)
- Integrates [**Tarmogoyf**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b6876d9e-0908-43ac-8542-09c7aa02b5ba?format=image) as the primary hyper-efficient attacker to close out games
- Features [**Trinket Mage**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4c5a41ab-1840-4abb-a8bb-f0b1e7d1b450?format=image) to search 1-mana or 0-mana artifacts directly from the library
- Utilizes [**Thirst for Knowledge**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image) as the primary draw engine and its discard clause pulls double duty by fueling the graveyard to rapidly inflate [**Tarmogoyf**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b6876d9e-0908-43ac-8542-09c7aa02b5ba?format=image)’s power.
- [**Threads of Disloyalty**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/REPLACE_WITH_APPROPRIATE_UUID?format=image) to steal and capitalize on elite, high-impact resolved creatures like [**Tarmogoyf**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b6876d9e-0908-43ac-8542-09c7aa02b5ba?format=image) and [**Dark Confidant**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/94f7a441-bf2d-46fb-a7b6-9bd6137f86d9?format=image)
