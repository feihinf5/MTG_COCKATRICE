Note
- Japan Legacy Championship 2009 winning deck by Sasou Kazuho
- Cast [**Counterbalance**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/c329ff2b-0331-4934-a8df-870dd7bf402b?format=image) and pair it with [**Sensei's Divining Top**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4a08ca06-58db-4ce6-b490-be4bea8956a1?format=image) to lock out the opponent
- Clear any existing wide board with [**Firespout**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/13454f69-1458-4c03-ab02-bd697a32eb17?format=image)
- Drop a [**Tarmogoyf**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b6876d9e-0908-43ac-8542-09c7aa02b5ba?format=image) or [**Rhox War Monk**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/19fb67e1-b791-4246-aebc-49fcf0f92c6c?format=image), attack while maintaining the Counter-Top lock to ensure your opponent cannot draw an answer
