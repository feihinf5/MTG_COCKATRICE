Format
- LEA <--> M10

Note
- Japan Legacy Championship 2009 winning deck by Sasou Kazuho
- Cast [**Counterbalance**] and pair it with [**Sensei's Divining Top**] to lock out the opponent
- Clear any existing wide board with [**Firespout**]
- Drop a [**Tarmogoyf**] or [**Rhox War Monk**], attack while maintaining the Counter-Top lock to ensure your opponent cannot draw an answer
