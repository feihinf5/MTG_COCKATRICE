Note
- 2009 Legacy Championship winning deck by Ernest Turck
- Cast Draw Spell, [**Breakthrough**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/a59a2cea-3b65-43da-bc6e-0e3c82f25b3c?format=image) or [**Cephalid Coliseum**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/d5d74112-7244-4c3f-a5eb-b6be671aefe8?format=image) while the spell is on the stack, you crack [**Lion's Eye Diamond**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/63bacc32-d6ba-420c-9b49-299c08e5fb39?format=image) to discard [**Dread Return**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/d7e304fc-0ace-459e-8d2f-376f1899639c?format=image)
- Replace every draw with [**Dread Return**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/d7e304fc-0ace-459e-8d2f-376f1899639c?format=image) to put 15-30 cards in Graveyard
- Free creatures [**Narcomoeba**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/f76b3746-2e2c-4560-a2d2-e7b5b92833b2?format=image) enter battlefield + Tokens spawn from [**Bridge from Below**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/52c44610-6d4b-4c14-839f-2c085badec90?format=image)
- Flashback [**Dread Return**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/d7e304fc-0ace-459e-8d2f-376f1899639c?format=image) target [**Flame-Kin Zealot**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/aec53f16-5bf7-4ef5-b83e-9e36463d1640?format=image) with Haste for win
- Could discard [**Dread Return**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/d7e304fc-0ace-459e-8d2f-376f1899639c?format=image) via [**Putrid Imp**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/1b9e6c5c-4bc4-4f41-8c2c-f5b8c97c53c5?format=image)
- [**Ichorid**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/97431dca-54ca-47ef-ab00-943140e8e758?format=image) returns from your graveyard at the start of your upkeep for free by exiling a black creature
- Reanimate [**Sadistic Hypnotist**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/98a6fba0-e5fc-4fa8-9895-8e4b8272bebe?format=image) and sacrifice your free creatures to force your opponent to discard their entire hand
- [**Cabal Therapy**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0a5df970-c6ba-4824-b8ba-67244aec2b82?format=image) to counterspells or graveyard hate in the opponent's hand before going off
