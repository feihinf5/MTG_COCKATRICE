Note
- World Championships 2009 6-0 Extended format deck by William Cavaglieri
- Sacrifice [**Sword of the Meek**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e9f13705-6ede-4c29-a2b4-a082bf69e9c5?format=image) to [**Thopter Foundry**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/42b8d797-b01d-49cf-9818-d84bba17029d?format=image) to gain 1 life and create a 1/1 Flying Thopter artifact creature
- Because a 1/1 creature entered the battlefield, [**Sword of the Meek**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e9f13705-6ede-4c29-a2b4-a082bf69e9c5?format=image) automatically returns from your graveyard attached to it
- [**Tezzeret the Seeker**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/3b214b6f-4734-4200-8467-92d7e3469b5d?format=image) -X Ability acts as a direct-to-battlefield tutor, X=2 puts [**Thopter Foundry**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/42b8d797-b01d-49cf-9818-d84bba17029d?format=image) directly into play or X=2 can also grab [**Sword of the Meek**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e9f13705-6ede-4c29-a2b4-a082bf69e9c5?format=image) (which goes to the graveyard when sacrificed anyway) or X=0 fetches artifact lands ([**Seat of the Synod**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5da5587d-6b6c-4645-8cc9-2866d1e6911b?format=image), [**Ancient Den**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/cc857fbd-8e0f-4bff-8f14-561c9925c484?format=image)) or [**Engineered Explosives**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/8492a272-e595-4f94-a6eb-08d29f211fd6?format=image)
- [**Muddle the Mixture**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/4cc785b0-0a77-4b02-b0b4-2bda2fc621cc?format=image) to fetch combo piece, [**Thopter Foundry**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/42b8d797-b01d-49cf-9818-d84bba17029d?format=image) or [**Sword of the Meek**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e9f13705-6ede-4c29-a2b4-a082bf69e9c5?format=image)
- [**Thirst for Knowledge**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0ff1f608-203e-4413-8753-37fc49731c87?format=image) to dumping [**Sword of the Meek**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e9f13705-6ede-4c29-a2b4-a082bf69e9c5?format=image) into the graveyard
- [**Engineered Explosives**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/8492a272-e595-4f94-a6eb-08d29f211fd6?format=image) can be recurred every single turn using [**Academy Ruins**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/af09af65-0a3b-42df-b0fd-372e2158beac?format=image)
- [**Sword of the Meek**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e9f13705-6ede-4c29-a2b4-a082bf69e9c5?format=image) was banned in Extended format during July 2010 https://web.archive.org/web/20160117184748/http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/feature/95b
