Note
- World Championships 2009 6-0 Extended format deck by Przemyslaw Nagadowski
- [**Simian Spirit Guide**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5d7f701c-dcdc-4067-8d00-b4b7aadee9ba?format=image) exile from hand for a free red mana to cast a Cascade spell([**Violent Outburst**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/1b58856a-f88e-4625-8636-62b5c717b956?format=image), [**Ardent Plea**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9d521737-ee07-4387-bc07-5ced53db374d?format=image), [**Demonic Dread**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/fb81132e-ab33-435f-ade4-af4416d36044?format=image)) as early as Turn 2
- Every spell in the main deck costs 3 mana or more, Cascade spell could only hit [**Hypergenesis**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/da4d6969-e579-4b28-a76b-5a99c7488f15?format=image) (which has a Mana Value of 0) and when it resolves, could play any threat from hand ([**Progenitus**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/bcc764b0-3046-4bde-b424-c0f4e1a6169b?format=image), [**Bogardan Hellkite**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5852f528-92aa-45cf-8436-5774691676d3?format=image), [**Angel of Despair**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9ae71424-df9c-481c-9305-a0c30adcfda2?format=image), [**Akroma, Angel of Fury**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/3815de82-5ba9-4fb6-9259-d5e50e046890?format=image), [**Sakashima the Impostor**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/61dc2f54-3637-4caa-9741-36ff14dc5527?format=image))
- [**Hypergenesis**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/da4d6969-e579-4b28-a76b-5a99c7488f15?format=image) was banned in Extended format during July 2010 https://web.archive.org/web/20160117184748/http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/feature/95b
