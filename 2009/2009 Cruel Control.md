Note
- 2009 United States National Championship winning deck by Charles Gindy
- The Ultimate Finisher [**Cruel Ultimatum**]
- Because a [**Vivid Creek**] or [**Vivid Marsh**] can produce any color by using a charge counter, [**Reflecting Pool**] turns into a untapped land that produces all 5 colors of mana unconditionally
- Filter Lands ([**Cascade Bluffs**], [**Mystic Gate**], [**Sunken Ruins**]): Convert double generic/single colors into precise color pairs on demand
- [**Cryptic Command**] offered pure flexibility (Counter/Draw, Tap team/Draw, Bounce/Counter)
- [**Esper Charm**] acted as an instant-speed "Draw 2", destroyed key enchantments or forced the opponent to discard 2 cards at their draw step
- [**Volcanic Fallout**], uncounterable instant-speed mass damage that wiped away aggressive Faeries and Token decks without needing to mainphase a sorcery sweeper
- [**Plumeveil**] acted as a surprise 4/4 Flash blocker to blow out attackers while [**Broodmate Dragon**] created two 4/4 flyers in a single cast, putting 8 flying power on board
