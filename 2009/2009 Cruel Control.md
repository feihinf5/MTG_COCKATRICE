Note
- 2009 United States National Championship winning deck by Charles Gindy
- The Ultimate Finisher [**Cruel Ultimatum**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0d267804-23de-4521-ae64-4dd5cd836c6c?format=image)
- Because a [**Vivid Creek**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/2e3c2935-84ee-446d-b247-2f574ea84a8f?format=image) or [**Vivid Marsh**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/31f806c7-0063-4270-b37c-5363c41a7621?format=image) can produce any color by using a charge counter, [**Reflecting Pool**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/3d03dc37-03fb-47b2-aa25-60a85d0d94dd?format=image) turns into a untapped land that produces all 5 colors of mana unconditionally
- Filter Lands ([**Cascade Bluffs**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/c3eede44-270a-481d-850b-b4862b9685ea?format=image), [**Mystic Gate**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/3dfa866b-93e2-4365-91b0-f12d1f7c5395?format=image), [**Sunken Ruins**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9d91a31c-b70a-45bd-a8dd-48d49b277f24?format=image)): Convert double generic/single colors into precise color pairs on demand
- [**Cryptic Command**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/829e3d6e-5d7c-4cc4-a7a6-7cbf5a7442ba?format=image) offered pure flexibility (Counter/Draw, Tap team/Draw, Bounce/Counter)
- [**Esper Charm**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/a3d70a07-8d91-462c-aa96-901cc9a81531?format=image) acted as an instant-speed "Draw 2", destroyed key enchantments or forced the opponent to discard 2 cards at their draw step
- [**Volcanic Fallout**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/65536d12-e75c-42b5-b592-a3ad4f550a71?format=image), uncounterable instant-speed mass damage that wiped away aggressive Faeries and Token decks without needing to mainphase a sorcery sweeper
- [**Plumeveil**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/8e8b0449-3ae5-4fee-b870-af12be5e5a64?format=image) acted as a surprise 4/4 Flash blocker to blow out attackers while [**Broodmate Dragon**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/aea1360e-7c6b-400c-893a-82d93e53101e?format=image) created two 4/4 flyers in a single cast, putting 8 flying power on board
