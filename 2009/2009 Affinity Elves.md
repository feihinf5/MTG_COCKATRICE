Note
- 2009 Australia National Championship winning deck by Jamie Mackintosh
- [**Heritage Druid**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/a726acbd-724e-46c5-a4cf-4aee7c2abb16?format=image) + [**Nettle Sentinel**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/f9f21681-fd36-4106-8395-3153599a08a6?format=image) allowing you to continuously generate mana out of nowhere by casting 1-mana green creatures
- [**Devoted Druid**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/820e2f07-f637-4144-b45a-0e1430dcf55e?format=image) + [**Elvish Archdruid**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/544bc214-e32d-4370-a15f-62812a0420be?format=image) provide massive bursts of green mana to pay for big spells early
- [**Regal Force**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/674a694a-22b8-4fb8-b901-91d64564c0db?format=image), the draw engine of the deck
- [**Ranger of Eos**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/1a30ee26-5f78-4ac2-9105-1baa9ece8a21?format=image) to search for [**Heritage Druid**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/a726acbd-724e-46c5-a4cf-4aee7c2abb16?format=image) and [**Nettle Sentinel**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/f9f21681-fd36-4106-8395-3153599a08a6?format=image) to assemble your combo loop instantly or grab utility cards like [**Burrenton Forge-Tender**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/c000c3e4-d71a-43c8-8ded-f3da54bc088d?format=image) or [**Llanowar Elves**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/199e047c-823b-4d18-a5e9-6de3e6989858?format=image)
- Tutor or draw into [**Mirror Entity**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/adfff880-cbf6-4085-bc05-c72658b75f25?format=image) and pay X mana to instantly turn all your 1/1 Elves into 10/10 or 20/20 giant creatures and swing for lethal damage in a single turn
- [**Windbrisk Heights**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9df6a31a-5c49-4506-b8f8-84c9ab4a2ece?format=image), attacking with 3 tiny Elves triggers Hideaway, letting you cast high-cost spells
- [**Cloudthresher**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/c759c561-829d-483d-a433-fe1213f20236?format=image) provides a tutorable answer to flying armies while triggering Nettle Sentinel untaps.
