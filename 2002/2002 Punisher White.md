Note
- World Championships 2002 Odyssey Block Constructed deck by Tomi Walamies
- core engine [**Quiet Speculation**] and [**Battle Screech**]
- Utilizes discard ability such as [**Tireless Tribe**], [**Patrol Hound**] and [**Aquamoeba**] to abuse Madness and Flashback mechanics
- Stalls for time using [**Prismatic Strands**] then rapidly closes out the game by swinging with white weenie creatures and the bird tokens generated by [**Battle Screech**]
