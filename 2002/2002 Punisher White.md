Note
- World Championships 2002 Odyssey Block Constructed deck by Tomi Walamies
- core engine [**Quiet Speculation**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/71a314fa-293b-486f-95d8-267d340e4d8e?format=image) and [**Battle Screech**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/c3c38264-0d79-47d4-bca2-a20a991bbac9?format=image)
- Utilizes discard ability such as [**Tireless Tribe**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/1d23e47a-21d5-4d7e-8aa0-3b3064da5967?format=image), [**Patrol Hound**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/e216a539-152b-4a83-98ef-1996182a5714?format=image) and [**Aquamoeba**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/1243552a-ca57-42ce-817e-d6268fc673e0?format=image) to abuse Madness and Flashback mechanics
- Stalls for time using [**Prismatic Strands**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/3454ef42-2e0b-4ce4-945f-e4ec3e83c39d?format=image) then rapidly closes out the game by swinging with white weenie creatures and the bird tokens generated by [**Battle Screech**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/c3c38264-0d79-47d4-bca2-a20a991bbac9?format=image)
