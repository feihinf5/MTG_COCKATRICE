Note
- Grand Prix-Taipei 2002 winning deck by Hsia Sheng Hsum
- Built around [**Hunting Grounds**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5b14a736-5223-457b-9d4e-f4a2d6ed9a8d?format=image) that allow creatures onto the battlefield for free under specific conditions
- Control the board using [**Counterspell**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/29bb1b85-9444-4bfa-b622-092a6873631c?format=image), [**Memory Lapse**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/2d85cc30-ccae-4af8-834a-f7870dace679?format=image) and [**Wrath of God**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/0d223e83-0d3c-459e-96f5-ba9227fe49dd?format=image) then once mana is established and Threshold is achieved, [**Hunting Grounds**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5b14a736-5223-457b-9d4e-f4a2d6ed9a8d?format=image) is deployed
- [**Mystic Snake**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/f098a28c-5f9b-4a2c-b109-c342365eb948?format=image) put into play via [**Hunting Grounds**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/5b14a736-5223-457b-9d4e-f4a2d6ed9a8d?format=image) is repeatedly reused using [**Repulse**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9a04e9be-48be-440e-9825-cfffd4c2b1a4?format=image) or [**Genesis**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/b43aee5e-b12e-43ea-9fae-16310acdc640?format=image) to naturally generate massive card advantage
- [**Nantuko Monastery**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/cb870406-9d59-4493-9f81-0f4b84642001?format=image) is universally run as a utility land
- Highly effective against to counter a lethal [**Upheaval**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/9e201229-34a6-48c8-a07c-d8aefcf5f8a7?format=image)
- To guarantee achieving Threshold, the deck utilized [**Fact or Fiction**](https://api.scryfall.com/cards/7fd4d018-dcf3-4439-8445-02d66e44f7d3?format=image)
